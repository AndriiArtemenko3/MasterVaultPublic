## 1. New tier

12% off list at 30 or more units, effective 2025-12-08. This replaces the 15% at 25+ tier that ran from 2024-06-01.

## 2. Why now

The 2025 margin review found the average realized B2B discount running above plan for two straight quarters. Raising the unit floor from 25 to 30 and cutting the rate from 15% to 12% brings realized margin back toward plan without touching list prices.

## 3. Signed deals keep their pricing

This tier governs quotes issued from 2025-12-08 forward. Anything already signed keeps the pricing on its contract. Order #LS31695, Cobalt Dental Group, closed 2025-11-14 at the 15% tier, is unaffected; nobody re-bills that deal.

## 4. Exception code unchanged

The SL3 exception code stands as written: a sales lead may grant a one-time extra 3% on deals of 30 or more seats, and CEO sign-off is required once the exception's dollar value reaches 1,000.00, per the expense policy.
