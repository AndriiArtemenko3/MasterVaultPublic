Competitor: ErgoNest
Card revised: 2024-08-19 by TA
Prior revision: none
Sources: two showroom walk-ins, names withheld, week of 2024-08-05; ergonest site checked 2024-08-16.

## Positioning

Direct-import reseller of white-label ergonomic desks and chairs, competing on price and shipping speed rather than fit or repairability. Both walk-ins this month brought the name up first, unprompted. That hadn't happened before.

## Price posture

Consistent with the 10-18% band we've been quoting verbally. No new dated figure to log this cycle, both leads wanted to talk about returns rather than price.

## Where they win

- lower sticker across the desk-and-chair pairing
- 2-day shipping, western US
- a wider chair range, worth its own look next revision

## Where we win

- the parts shelf. Caster set at 24.00, armrest pads at 29.00, a motor control box under ninety, all published SKUs a customer can order without calling us first
- a longer return window than theirs. One lead said the fourteen-day window nearly cost her a refund outright when her ErgoNest delivery ran late and she didn't notice the clock had run out until she called
- the second lead said a restocking charge got applied to what she'd assumed was an unopened return, and the charge wasn't disclosed until after the return was already in motion, not before

## Ground rules

Compare terms, never speculate about finances or build quality we haven't tested ourselves. Nothing in writing to a prospect names them. Customer paper says "another direct-import option."
