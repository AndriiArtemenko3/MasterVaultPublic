Analysis: B2B pipeline by stage, first six months
Date: 2024-12-03
Author: Tom Aldridge
Data: Pipewell export pulled 2024-12-02; every B2B account contacted since my
2024-06-03 start date.

## Question

Six months in, where does each account actually sit: closed, open, or not yet touched?

## Table

| Stage | Accounts | Seats | First contact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Closed-won | 2 | 30 | Jul, Sep 2024 |
| Proposal | 1 | 12 | Oct 2024 |
| Discovery | 1 | 18 | Nov 2024 |

## Findings

1. Harborview Architects closed 2024-09-19 at 22 seats, six dual monitor arms
   stacked on top of the desk-and-chair order because the studio wanted every
   drafting table paired with two screens, not one. First contact to signature
   ran about ten weeks.
2. Summit Physio Group closed 2024-11-06 at 8 seats and already put in a
   Finch footrest reorder before the month was out. That's the fast second
   order I want to see more of, and it came from a five-week cycle, half the
   length of Harborview's.
3. Stonebridge Accounting, 12 seats, sits in proposal. Their partner keeps
   pushing the start date back a week at a time, so this one might slip into
   January before it's signed. I'd rather it slip than get rushed into a
   floor plan nobody's checked.
4. Beacon Legal, 18 seats, is discovery only. One call booked, nothing
   written yet, no commitment either way. Too early to call this one at all.
5. All four came from outbound calling. The showroom hasn't sent a single
   B2B lead my way yet in six months, which either means foot traffic skews
   B2C or it means nobody at the front desk is asking the right question
   when a business owner walks in.

## Caveats

Four accounts total. Two closed doesn't make a close rate. It makes a start.
The gap between discovery and proposal is one phone call right now, so don't
read process maturity into that either; with four data points every account
is its own case study, not a pattern, and the cycle-length numbers above are
each an n of one. Seat count also isn't seat count times price yet: nothing
here says what any of these three paid or will pay, since that's a separate
conversation and this memo is stage only.

Next: first follow-up call with Beacon Legal's decision-maker by 2024-12-18,
and a check-in with Stonebridge's partner on the slipping start date by
2024-12-20. Owner: TA.
