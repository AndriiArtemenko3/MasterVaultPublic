Owner: Dmitri Okafor (DO), QC partner Hank Morrow (HM)
Opened: 2024-09-03   Target close: 2024-11-22
Related: VEN-02, LOT-2024-41

Goal
Launch LS-ACC-007 on 2024-11-11 with rail QC cleared at a 98% cycle-test pass rate across a 150-unit sample pulled from the first production run before the SKU goes live in Shopstack.

This is Ostrava's (VEN-02) first drawer-hardware part for us, not just another bracket off an existing die, so the tooling lead time runs longer than a normal accessory add. Sales has been asking for an under-desk drawer since spring. The catalog gap is real: nowhere in the parts shelf to stash a charger brick or a stack of sticky notes.

Plan
- Tooling: Ostrava cuts a new rail bracket die, first-article samples due 24 Sep, full sign-off by 08 Oct.
- QC plan: pull 150 units from LOT-2024-41's first production run, run each on Hank's bench through 5,000 open-close cycles, flag any bind or rail separation past 4,000 cycles.
- Fit check: mount 3 sample units under each of the three Birch desk widths, LS-DSK-001-48, -60, and -72, to confirm the bracket clears the center leg on all three.
- Inventory: land 300 units by 30 Oct ahead of the 11 Nov launch. 150 held back for the QC pull, 150 released straight to stock.

Risks
- Rail bracket die comes back oversized on first article: fallback is a second tooling pass with a 15 Oct backstop before the 150-unit run has to slip past October entirely.
- Bracket doesn't clear the 48 in desk's center leg: fallback is a shim kit boxed in for that width only, a decision Hank and Dmitri lock before launch, not after.
- Ostrava's line is already carrying the Rowan chair base order that same month: fallback is Dmitri holds the drawer run's slot with a signed PO by 20 Sep so it doesn't get bumped behind the chair order if both compete for the same shift.
