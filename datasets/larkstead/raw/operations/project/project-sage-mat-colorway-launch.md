Project: Alder mat sage colorway launch, LS-MAT-001-SGE
Owner: Dmitri Okafor (DO)
Opened: 2024-01-15   Target close: 2024-02-19
Related: VEN-01, LS-MAT-001-SGE, LS-MAT-001-CHL, LS-MAT-001-SND

Goal
Launch a third Alder desk mat colorway, sage, on Shopstack by 2024-02-19 at
49.00, with 1,200 units landed at the Portland backroom and matched to the
approved swatch within one shade before the listing goes live.

Plan
- Swatch round: Verdant Textiles (VEN-01) dyes four sage candidates against
  a reference chip Sofia pulled from a moss sample she'd been carrying around
  since December. Two greener, two more muted; side by side against the
  existing charcoal and sand mats for scale, not against a screen.
- Sofia and Mara pick sage 3, the muted moss option, over sage 1 and sage 4
  on the grounds that it reads calmer next to the birch desk finish.
- Production lot LOT-2024-05: 1,200 units, dyed to the sage 3 standard,
  inbound via Cascadia Freight (VEN-06) since Verdant ships out of Vietnam
  on the same lane as the other Alder colors.
- Landing at the Portland backroom, not ParcelPoint. Mats are a light-weight
  SKU and, same as every other light item this year, they ship out of the
  showroom backroom until the ParcelPoint outbound cutover, whenever that
  finally lands.
- Pricing matches the existing two colors exactly: 49.00 list, 14.50 landed
  cost per unit, no premium for the new color.
- Sofia builds the Shopstack listing and a short Mailloft send, three colors
  side by side, no discount attached, just the new option.

Risks
- Sage 3 doesn't match the approved chip once the full 1,200-unit lot is
  dyed, not just the four samples: fallback pushes launch to 2024-02-26 and
  holds the lot at Verdant for a re-dye rather than shipping an off-match run.
- Cascadia inbound slips past 12 Feb on customs paperwork: fallback lists
  sage as available-to-order with a five business day ship estimate rather
  than moving the 19 Feb date.
- Verdant substitutes a different dye house mid-lot without flagging it:
  fallback is Hank's team pulling a five-unit spot sample against the
  reference chip at receiving, before the lot clears into stock.

Log
15 Jan: kickoff. Swatch request sent to Verdant, four candidates asked for,
Sofia's moss chip attached as the target.
22 Jan: four swatches in. Sage 1 and sage 4 read too bright next to charcoal
and sand on the showroom shelf.
25 Jan: sage 3 approved by Sofia and Mara. LOT-2024-05 confirmed at 1,200
units, Cascadia booking requested same day.
05 Feb: LOT-2024-05 departs Verdant.
12 Feb: LOT-2024-05 received at the Portland backroom. 1,200 units, 24
cartons of 50. Hank's spot check against the reference chip is clean, no
re-dye needed.
19 Feb: sage live on Shopstack at 49.00, alongside charcoal and sand.
Mailloft send goes out same afternoon.
