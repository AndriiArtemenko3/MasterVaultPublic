Goal
Launch LS-BDL-003 on 2025-01-20 with a 25-kit pilot batch assembled clean at ParcelPoint (VEN-04) and zero component shortages at go-live.

Plan
- Component list locked 07 Oct after Tom's B2B calls turned up the same two asks over and over from prospects sizing a 72 in desk tier: dual monitor arm, and something better than a desk lamp from a home-goods aisle. Final six: 72 in Birch desk (LS-DSK-001-72), black Rowan chair (LS-CHR-001-BLK), dual Heron arm (LS-ARM-001-DBL), Vireo lamp (LS-LMP-001), charcoal Alder mat (LS-MAT-001-CHL), Magpie cable tray (LS-ACC-003).

- Sourcing: desk frames and chair bases share one Ostrava run, LOT-2024-51, ordered 07 Oct for a 20 Nov landing. Arm castings run separately against LOT-2024-53 since Ostrava's arm line was already booked through October, landing 05 Dec. Lamp units pull from Meridian's LOT-2024-49, landing 12 Dec. Mats pull from Verdant's LOT-2025-02, the tightest of the four, landing 02 Jan.
- Kitting: ParcelPoint assembles a 25-kit pilot the week of 13 Jan, 6.00 per kit, 150.00 total, ahead of the 20 Jan launch.
- Pilot order #LS63600 built and shipped 15 Jan to confirm the bundle explodes correctly into six line items on the pick sheet rather than shipping as one SKU.

Four vendors, four lot numbers, one launch date. Any single component landing late pushes the whole bundle, so the sourcing column got its own tracker separate from this one starting late October.

Risks
- Verdant's mat lot lands after the kitting week: fallback is kitting the other five components and adding mats in a same-week top-up before the 20 Jan launch, one day of cushion built into the pilot schedule for exactly this.
- ParcelPoint's kitting queue backs up from holiday-return volume in early January: fallback is Dmitri books the 25-kit pilot as a priority slot two weeks out rather than first-come.
