Project: Portland showroom refresh, demo stations and pickup counter
Owner: Dmitri Okafor (DO)
Opened: 2024-04-01   Target close: 2024-05-31
Related: VEN-08

Goal
Rebuild the Portland showroom floor by 2024-05-31: three desk-size demo
stations, two suggested-setup displays, and a pickup counter moved off the
back wall, without closing the floor to walk-in traffic for more than two
weekdays total.

Plan
- Demo station 1: 48 in Birch desk, graphite Rowan chair, Wren stand. 549.00
  plus 389.00 plus 69.00.
- Demo station 2: 60 in Birch desk, fog gray Rowan chair, single Heron arm.
  649.00 plus 389.00 plus 129.00.
- Demo station 3: 72 in Birch desk, graphite Rowan chair, dual Heron arm,
  Finch footrest. 749.00 plus 389.00 plus 189.00 plus 69.00.
- Two suggested-setup displays, priced as separate line items rather than a
  bundle since there isn't one yet: a 48 in desk with graphite chair and a
  Sparrow cable kit, and a 60 in desk with graphite chair, single Heron arm,
  and a Sparrow cable kit. Sofia wants to see whether customers ask for the
  set as one line before anyone commits to actually pricing it that way.
- Pickup counter moves from the back wall to just inside the entrance, with
  a two-stool waiting nook and a Shopstack lookup terminal so a walk-in
  pickup doesn't have to cross the whole floor to get flagged down.
- Hank's team builds all three stations and the counter on a Monday and
  Tuesday close, floor reopens Wednesday morning.

Risks
- Lovejoy Property Partners (VEN-08) sign-off on the wall-mounted Heron arm
  anchors slips past the build weekend: fallback is floor-standing clamp
  mounts on both display stations instead of wall brackets.
- Combined materials and build labor land near 1,840.00, over the 1,000.00
  CEO approval line: fallback, if Mara doesn't sign off before the ordered
  materials arrive, is holding the counter rework and running the desk
  stations on the original date alone.
- Floor closed longer than two days pushes into a weekend the showroom
  usually sees walk-in traffic: fallback moves the close to a slower week
  rather than eating a Saturday.
