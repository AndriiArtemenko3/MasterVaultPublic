Process: Marketing campaign launch
Effective: 2024-09-03
Owner: Sofia Grieg   Teams: marketing, support
Trigger: a campaign brief approved for send

Stages
| # | stage | owner | system | exit criteria |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | naming | SG | n/a | campaign assigned a weather name not reused within the current year |
| 2 | list selection | SG | Mailloft | send list built, segment counts confirmed against the brief |
| 3 | copy and offer | SG | Mailloft | subject line under 6 words; offer terms checked against the policy in force |
| 4 | support heads-up | SG | email | Priya's team notified 3 business days before send, with offer terms and expected volume |
| 5 | send | SG | Mailloft | campaign sent at the scheduled time; send confirmation logged |
| 6 | results | SG | Mailloft | open and click counts pulled 48 hours after send |

Rules
- Weather names don't repeat inside a calendar year. Sofia keeps the running list, and nobody else gets to name a send.
- Any dollar-off or free-shipping call-out matches the policy in force on the send date. The threshold sat at 75.00 from 2024-03-01 onward, and nothing below that line ever goes out as a promotion.
- Heads-up to support means 3 full business days, not calendar days. A Thursday send needs the notice out the Monday before, not Tuesday.
- List selection checks segment before the send list locks. B2B contacts stay off consumer weather campaigns, full stop, every single time.

Example run (Squall campaign, October 2024)
2024-09-25: brief approved for a general fall desk-refresh push across the catalog.
2024-09-26: named Squall, checked against the year's list. Drizzle ran in April, Chinook in July, so Squall was clear.
2024-09-27: list built in Mailloft, 4,206 B2C contacts, B2B segment excluded at the build step rather than filtered after.
2024-09-30: copy locked. Subject line "Mornings just got easier," five words. Offer: free shipping on orders over 75.00, matching the threshold in force.
2024-10-01: heads-up sent to Priya, three business days ahead of the planned Thursday send, offer terms and expected volume both in the note.
2024-10-04: sent at 8am.
2024-10-06: results pulled. Open rate ran ahead of the year's average for a catalog-wide push, and click-through held about even with Chinook.

Results logged, campaign closed.
