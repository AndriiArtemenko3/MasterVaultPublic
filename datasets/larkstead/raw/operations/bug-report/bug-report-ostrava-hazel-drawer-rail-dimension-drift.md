Bug report: ostrava-hazel-drawer-rail-dimension-drift
Date: 2026-04-21
Filed by: Dmitri Okafor (DO)
Filed with: Ostrava Metalworks (VEN-02)
System under report: Hazel under-desk drawer, LS-ACC-007, drawer rail
component, LOT-2026-09
Related tickets: 0 to date, caught at receiving, no units released to stock

Summary
300 units of LS-ACC-007 came in on LOT-2026-09, 13 Apr. A full-lot rail
check turned up 12 units running long on the slide rail, past the printed
tolerance, so those 12 are going back to Ostrava. The other 288 cleared.

Reproduction steps
1. Pull the entire LOT-2026-09 for a rail dimension check rather than a
   sample. A long rail binds the slide track, which is a functional fail,
   not cosmetic, so a sample pass here isn't good enough.
2. Measure both rails on each drawer against spec: 17.75 in plus or minus
   0.05 in.
3. On 12 of the 300 units, one or both rails read 17.83 to 17.94 in, past
   the 17.80 in ceiling.
4. Test-fit a flagged unit into a Hazel housing. The drawer binds partway
   open rather than sliding clean to the stop.

Affected population
LOT-2026-09, 300 units of LS-ACC-007, received 13 Apr. Full-lot check
completed 20 Apr by Hank's team. 12 of 300 units, 4 percent, measured past
the 17.80 in ceiling on one or both rails. 288 units cleared and released to
stock 21 Apr.

Evidence
The 12 flagged units clustered together in the pallet arrangement rather
than scattering evenly across the lot, which points to a die or fixture
drifting partway through the run instead of a spec problem across the whole
lot. The 288 cleared units read within 0.03 in of spec center, tight. Six
units pulled from existing shelf stock for comparison this week don't
reproduce the bind, so the field-installed base is not affected. No
customer impact, none shipped.

Ask
Return the 12 flagged units to Ostrava for credit against the next invoice.
Want written confirmation from them on the rail-tool calibration before the
next Hazel run ships, ahead of our next scheduled receipt in mid-May. If
they can't confirm calibration in time, I'd rather hold that shipment than
take a fourth lot on the same tooling unchecked.
