## Fit and sizing

Q: What laptop sizes fit the Wren stand?
A: Anything from a small 10 inch tablet-laptop hybrid up to a 17 inch gaming laptop sits fine on the platform, since the deck itself is 13 inches wide and most laptops rest across it rather than filling it edge to edge. Machines wider than about 15.5 inches will overhang the sides slightly, which is normal and doesn't affect stability.

Q: What's the weight limit?
A: 33 pounds. No laptop on the market comes close to that on its own, so the limit really only matters if someone stacks a laptop, a hub, and other gear on top at once.

## Grip and stability

Q: Will my laptop slide around on the stand?
A: The deck has a textured non-slip pad built into the surface, and as long as the pad is clean, laptops stay put even when you're typing on an external keyboard set up below at an angle. A laptop with a smooth ceramic or glass-back finish grips slightly less than an aluminum one; if yours slides, wipe the pad down before assuming it's a design issue.

Q: How do I clean the non-slip pad?
A: A slightly damp cloth with a drop of dish soap, wiped in one direction, gets dust and oil off without leaving the pad sticky. Skip glass cleaner and anything alcohol based; it breaks down the texture over time and the pad gets slicker instead of grippier.

## Setup

Q: Does it fold flat for travel?
A: Yes, the two side arms fold in against the base and it lays flat enough to slide into most laptop bag pockets.

Q: Do I need any tools to set it up?
A: None. The stand ships fully assembled; unfold the arms, set it on the desk, and it's ready.
