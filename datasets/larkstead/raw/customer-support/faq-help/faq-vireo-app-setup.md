## Creating your account

Q: Do I need to make an account before I can use the lamp?
A: Yes. Download the Vireo app, tap Create account, and use the same email you used on your Larkstead order if you want the two linked automatically. Used a different email at checkout? Sign up anyway, then add your #LS order number under Settings, Registered products once you're logged in.

Q: I already have a storefront account. Does that log me into the app too?
A: No, they're separate logins right now. Your storefront password won't carry over, so pick a new one the first time you open the app.

## Wi-Fi pairing

Q: The app can't find my lamp (LS-LMP-001) during setup. What's wrong?
A: Almost always the Wi-Fi band. The lamp only joins 2.4 GHz networks, so if your router broadcasts one combined network on 5 GHz, or your home network is 5 GHz only, pairing will time out no matter how many times you retry it. Look in your router settings for a separate 2.4 GHz network name, or set up a 2.4 GHz guest network just for smart-home devices if your router won't split the bands.

Q: How close does the lamp need to be to the router during setup?
A: Within about 15 feet, one wall at most. Once it joins your network you can move it anywhere in normal Wi-Fi range afterward.

Q: Setup says "pairing failed" right after I enter my Wi-Fi password. Now what?
A: Check the password for case sensitivity first, then hold the base dial for 5 seconds to reset pairing mode and try again. Force-quit the app before a third attempt; a stuck Bluetooth handshake on the phone side is the usual culprit.

## Scenes

Q: What's a scene?
A: A saved brightness and color-temperature combination you can jump to in one tap instead of moving both sliders every time. Three come loaded: Focus, a bright cool white for daytime work, Wind down, a dim warm setting for evenings, and Call, flatter and a touch brighter for video.

Q: Can I build my own?
A: Yes, up to 8 per lamp. Set the brightness and color temperature how you want them, tap the plus icon on the Scenes tab, and name it.

Q: Can I switch scenes from the base dial instead of the app?
A: One tap cycles through your saved scenes in order. A long press jumps back to whatever brightness you last set by hand.
