--- Message 2 (agent, public reply) --- 2024-03-14 14:38 PT
Hi Marcus, thanks for flagging this instead of just living with it. What
you're describing, the short hum right at the top of the travel range, is
the motor's duty cycle protection kicking in. Near the very end of a full
raise the motor briefly pauses and lets out that hum to keep itself from
overheating on a long run, and it settles once the desk reaches height. It's
expected behavior, especially on a 60 in frame carrying more weight over more
travel distance, and it isn't a sign of anything failing. If the sound ever
gets louder, changes pitch, or the desk starts hesitating before it reaches
full height, that would be worth a second look, but nothing you've described
points that direction.
