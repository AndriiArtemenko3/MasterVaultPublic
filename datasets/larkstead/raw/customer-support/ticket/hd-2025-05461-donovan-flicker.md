--- Message 2 (agent, public reply) --- 2025-12-15 20:24 PT
Hi Kit. Your order #LS31710 shows firmware v1.2, auto-installed on 2025-12-12, matching other flicker reports we've had recently.

You're not getting lucky. Disabling the sleep-mode dimming schedule in the app is a real workaround, it stops the lamp from stepping down to the low brightness where the flicker happens. The tradeoff is your brightness stays fixed instead of easing down at night.

I've logged this for our team to pass along to other affected customers while we work with the firmware vendor on a permanent fix.
