Title: My chair sinks down while I'm sitting in it
Product: Rowan task chair, both colors (LS-CHR-001-BLK, LS-CHR-001-GRY)
Applies to: every Rowan chair regardless of purchase date. The gas lift cylinder is the same part in both colors and hasn't changed since the 2023-02-01 launch.
Owner: Jonah Beck
Created: 2024-05-20
Last revised: 2024-05-20

## Symptom

Seat height drops on its own while seated: sometimes a noticeable half inch in the first few minutes, sometimes not obvious until the customer stands up at the end of the day and finds the chair has settled several inches lower than they set it that morning.

## Before you start

Get the order number and roughly how many months since purchase. Ask whether the sinking happens no matter who sits in the chair, or only under one particular person's weight, since that split matters for the next step.

## Steps

1. Run a 10-minute load test. Set the seat to a comfortable height, sit normally, and check the height again after 10 minutes without touching the lever.
2. A drop under a quarter inch in that window is ordinary cushion settling, not a lift failure, and doesn't need further diagnosis.
3. With help, tip the chair on its side and look at the cylinder, the metal tube running from the seat mechanism down into the base. Wipe it dry with a cloth.
4. Check for a wet or oily film on the cylinder. Oil residue means the internal seal has failed and the whole cylinder needs replacing; a bone-dry cylinder that still failed the load test in step 1 usually means gas has leaked out of a sealed chamber, which looks identical from the outside either way, so don't rule it out just because the metal looks clean.
5. Rule out the base before ordering a cylinder. With the chair unweighted, push down hard on the seat once. A sharp drop that doesn't recover on its own points to the base or casters, not the lift, and needs a different fix entirely.

## If the steps do not fix it

The gas lift cylinder doesn't have its own SKU or listed price the way the caster set (LS-ACC-008) or the armrest pads (LS-ACC-009) do; it isn't part of the customer-facing parts shelf. Inside the standard 1-year warranty, effective 2024-01-15, support ships a replacement cylinder at no charge and sends a prepaid label for the failed one, arranged directly rather than through a self-serve order. Past the 1-year mark the usual path is a full chair replacement rather than a single-part order. Check with Priya before quoting anything to a customer outside warranty.
