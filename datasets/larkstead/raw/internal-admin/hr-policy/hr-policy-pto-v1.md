Policy: Paid time off
Effective: 2024-01-15
Supersedes: none
Approved by: Mara Voss
Prepared by: Ana Petrova
Applies to: all employees; part-time staff accrue pro rata

1. Allowance
Every employee accrues 15 days of paid time off per calendar year, starting from day one of employment rather than after a probationary period. Accrual runs at 1.25 days per month, so a full year of tenure banks the full 15 days by December, and a new hire who starts in April still earns their first day of PTO before the end of that same month. There is no waiting period. Part-time staff accrue on the same monthly rate, scaled to scheduled hours against a standard 40-hour week, and Ana posts the pro-rated figure in the employee's file at hire.

2. Fixed holidays
Larkstead observes 8 paid holidays: New Year's Day, Presidents Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, the day after Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day. A holiday landing on a weekend shifts to the nearest weekday at Mara's discretion, and the shifted dates for the coming year go out by email each December.

3. Carryover
Up to 5 unused PTO days carry into the next calendar year. Carried days expire March 31. Anything unused after that date is forfeited, no exceptions, and there is no cash-out option for expired days.

4. Requesting time off
For 1 or 2 days off, email your manager at least 3 business days ahead. For 3 or more consecutive days, give at least 10 business days of notice so the schedule can absorb the gap, especially in support and the warehouse where a missing shift is felt immediately. Managers confirm or push back within 3 business days of the request. Silence past that window counts as approved.

5. Recordkeeping
Ana tracks balances and posts a running total to each employee once a quarter, and any employee can ask for a mid-quarter balance check by emailing her directly rather than waiting for the scheduled update.
