Policy: Remote and hybrid work
Effective: 2024-03-04
Supersedes: none
Approved by: Mara Voss
Prepared by: Ana Petrova
Applies to: all employees; scope varies by role as stated below

1. Support roles
Support agents may work remote up to 3 days per week. The remaining 2 days are in the Portland office, since ticket handoffs, new-hire shadowing, and hands-on testing against the physical desk and chair samples are faster in person than over a call. Remote days are chosen with the support lead and posted on the shared calendar at least a week in advance.

2. Warehouse
Warehouse staff work on site 5 days a week. The role is physical: receiving, kitting, and pick-pack work cannot be done from home, so there is no remote allowance here, full stop.

3. Sales, marketing, and operations
These roles are hybrid at manager discretion. The same 3-day remote cap that applies to support is the default, though a manager may approve a different split for a specific week, for example around a trade show, a vendor visit, or a stretch of heavy travel, as long as the total stays reasonable and Mara is looped in on anything that runs more than 2 consecutive weeks away from the office.

4. Core hours
Regardless of location, everyone is reachable between 10:00 and 15:00 Pacific, Monday through Friday. Meetings inside that window are not optional because someone is remote that day.

5. Equipment and connectivity
Employees use company-issued laptops for all work, remote or in office. Home internet, a quiet space to take calls, and a reasonably reliable connection are the employee's own responsibility; Larkstead does not reimburse home internet bills under this policy.

6. Review
Mara reviews this policy against team feedback each quarter and can tighten or loosen the remote allowance for a given role without a full rewrite, so long as any change is logged below.
