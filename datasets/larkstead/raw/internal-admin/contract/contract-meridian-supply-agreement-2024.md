SUPPLY AGREEMENT

This supply agreement ("Agreement") is made 2024-02-01 (the "Effective Date") between Larkstead Goods Company, LLC ("Client") and Meridian Components ("Vendor", VEN-07), covering electronic components and assemblies Vendor supplies to Client by purchase order. This Agreement was reviewed for Client by Colefield & Tran LLP (VEN-10) prior to signing.

1. Definitions
1.1 "Products" means printed circuit board assemblies, cable kits, motor control components, power adapters, and related electronic parts that Vendor supplies under a purchase order issued by Client from time to time.
1.2 "Revision" means any change to a Product's bill of materials, circuit design, or manufacturing process that Vendor designates with a new revision letter or otherwise documents internally, however minor.
1.3 "First Article" means the initial production sample of a Product, or of a Product carrying a new Revision, submitted to Client for inspection and written approval before volume shipment begins.

2. Revision control
2.1 Vendor shall notify Client in writing at least 30 days before introducing a Revision to any Product then in active production for Client, identifying the Revision letter and describing the change in enough detail for Client's engineering staff to assess its effect on form, fit, and function.
2.2 Vendor shall not ship a Revision in volume until Client has issued written approval of the First Article for that Revision under Section 3.
2.3 Vendor shall maintain traceability from every shipped unit's serial number to its Revision letter and shall provide that mapping to Client within 5 business days of a written request.
2.4 A Revision introduced without the notice required by Section 2.1 entitles Client to reject the affected shipment at Vendor's cost, freight included.

3. First-article inspection rights
3.1 Client may inspect First Article samples at its Portland facility, or request inspection at Vendor's facility in Taiwan at Client's own travel expense.
3.2 Client has 10 business days from receipt of a First Article sample to approve or reject it in writing. No response within that window counts as rejection, not approval.
3.3 A rejected First Article may be corrected and resubmitted once; each resubmission restarts the 10-business-day review period in full.

4. Fees and payment
4.1 Pricing for each Product is set by purchase order at the time of order and is not fixed by this Agreement.
4.2 Vendor invoices Client on Net-45 terms from the invoice date.
4.3 A balance unpaid past Net-45 accrues interest at 1% per month until paid.

5. Term
5.1 This Agreement runs from the Effective Date for 2 years and renews automatically for successive 1-year terms unless either party gives the other 60 days' written notice of non-renewal before a term ends.

6. Order of precedence
6.1 A signed purchase order controls over this Agreement on price, quantity, and delivery date only. All other terms of this Agreement govern every purchase order issued under it.
