AMENDMENT NO. 1 TO MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This amendment ("Amendment") is made 2025-07-28 (the "Amendment Date") between Larkstead Goods Company, LLC ("Client") and ParcelPoint Fulfillment ("Vendor", VEN-04), and amends the master services agreement dated 2023-11-01 (the "Master Agreement") governing warehousing, bundle kitting, and heavy-item freight out of Vendor's Reno, NV facility. Client's internal spend approval was recorded under the expense policy effective 2025-07-01.

1. Definitions

1.1 "Full Outbound Fulfillment" means pick, pack, and ship of all Client parcels regardless of weight class, replacing the prior split under which light and medium parcels shipped from Client's Portland, Oregon showroom backroom.

1.2 "Exhibit B" means the weight-billing exhibit attached to this Amendment, governing how cartons are billed by weight class.

1.3 "Effective Date" means 2025-08-01, the date on which Full Outbound Fulfillment begins under this Amendment.

1.4 "Backroom Parcels" means the light and medium parcels previously packed and shipped from Client's Portland showroom backroom prior to the Effective Date.

2. Scope

2.1 Vendor's scope under the Master Agreement expands from warehousing, bundle kitting, and heavy-item freight to Full Outbound Fulfillment from the Reno, NV warehouse, effective as of the Effective Date.

2.2 Client will transition its parcel order feed to Vendor over a migration period preceding the Effective Date; the parties will coordinate the specific cutover date separately in writing once Client's integration work is complete.

2.3 The parties acknowledge that this expansion follows sustained growth in Client's order volume and constrained capacity in the Backroom Parcels workflow, both of which motivate consolidating all outbound fulfillment at Vendor's Reno facility.

2.4 Vendor remains responsible for inbound warehousing and bundle kitting as under the Master Agreement, unchanged by this Amendment except as to rates in Section 3.

3. Rate card

3.1 Pick-pack: 2.85 per order, plus 0.55 per additional item beyond the first in that order.

3.2 Bundle kitting: 6.00 per kit, unchanged from the Master Agreement.

3.3 Pallet storage: 22.50 per pallet-position per month.

3.4 Rates in this Section 3 supersede any conflicting rate in the Master Agreement as of the Effective Date.

4. Exhibit B: weight-class billing

4.1 Heavy weight-class cartons bill at actual scale weight. No dimensional-weight calculation applies to heavy cartons under any circumstance.

4.2 Dimensional weight, calculated with a divisor of 166, applies to light and medium parcels only.

4.3 Vendor will itemize the weight class and the calculation method used for every carton on the monthly invoice.

5. Payment terms

5.1 Payment terms remain Net-30 per the Master Agreement. Invoices continue under the INV-PPF-YYYY-NNN numbering already in use.

6. Order of precedence

6.1 This Amendment controls over the Master Agreement where the two conflict. All other terms of the Master Agreement stand.
