AMENDMENT NO. 1 TO FREIGHT SERVICES AGREEMENT

This amendment ("Amendment") is made 2025-02-10 (the "Amendment Date") between Larkstead Goods Company, LLC ("Client") and Cascadia Freight ("Carrier", VEN-06), and restates the rate schedule under the freight services agreement dated 2023-02-15 (the "Agreement") covering inbound drayage and LTL moves to the ParcelPoint warehouse.

1. Definitions
1.1 "Container Drayage" means inbound ocean container transport from the Port of Portland to the ParcelPoint warehouse in Reno, Nevada.
1.2 "LTL Shipment" means a less-than-truckload move of one or more pallets under the Agreement, other than a full Container Drayage move.
1.3 "Fuel Surcharge" means the percentage addition to line-haul charges on Container Drayage and LTL Shipments, calculated under Section 4.

2. Container drayage rate
2.1 Carrier bills Container Drayage at a flat rate of 1,850.00 per 40-foot container, inclusive of chassis and standard dwell time up to 48 hours from container release.
2.2 Dwell beyond 48 hours bills at 85.00 per additional day or part of a day.

3. LTL pallet-mile rate bands
3.1 Carrier bills each LTL Shipment per pallet, at the rate for the mileage band covering the shipment's origin-to-destination distance, subject to the band's minimum charge if the calculated amount falls below it:

    Band 1, 0 to 150 miles: 2.10 per pallet-mile, 175.00 minimum
    Band 2, 151 to 350 miles: 1.65 per pallet-mile, 320.00 minimum
    Band 3, 351 to 600 miles: 1.20 per pallet-mile, 480.00 minimum
    Band 4, 601 miles and over: 0.95 per pallet-mile, 640.00 minimum

3.2 Mileage is measured by Carrier's standard routing software at time of booking and stated on the rate confirmation Carrier sends Client before pickup.
3.3 A shipment of fewer than 3 pallets bills at the applicable band's minimum charge regardless of the per-pallet-mile calculation, unless the calculation itself exceeds the minimum.

4. Fuel surcharge formula
4.1 Carrier recalculates the Fuel Surcharge on the first Monday of each month from the U.S. Department of Energy West Coast average diesel price published that week.
4.2 The Fuel Surcharge equals 4 percent for every 0.25 by which that price exceeds a 3.20 per gallon baseline, rounded down to the nearest whole percent, with a floor of 0 percent and a cap of 25 percent.
4.3 Carrier states the Fuel Surcharge percentage in effect on the invoice for every shipment it applies to, and the percentage stays fixed for shipments booked that calendar month even if the published diesel price moves again before month end.

5. Payment terms
5.1 This Amendment changes no term of the Agreement other than the rates restated in Sections 2 through 4. Carrier continues to invoice on Net-30 terms.
