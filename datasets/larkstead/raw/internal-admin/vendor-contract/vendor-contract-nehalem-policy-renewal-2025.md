ANNUAL POLICY RENEWAL AGREEMENT

This renewal ("Renewal") is made 2025-07-01 (the "Effective Date") between
Larkstead Goods Company, LLC ("Insured") and Nehalem Mutual Insurance
("Insurer", VEN-09), and renews the commercial insurance policy between
the parties originally dated 2022-07-01 (the "Policy"), including the
product liability rider bound 2024-10-01 ahead of the Vireo smart desk
lamp launch.

1. Definitions
1.1 "Policy Period" means 2025-07-01 through 2026-06-30.
1.2 "Product Liability Rider" means the coverage added to the Policy on
    2024-10-01 for claims arising from the Vireo smart desk lamp
    (LS-LMP-001) product line, continued without lapse under this
    Renewal.
1.3 "Covered Premises" means Insured's 2,140 sq ft office and showroom
    space in NW Portland, Oregon.

2. Coverage limits
2.1 General liability: 1,000,000.00 per occurrence, 2,000,000.00 general
    aggregate.
2.2 Product Liability Rider: 1,000,000.00 per occurrence, 2,000,000.00
    aggregate, unchanged from the limits bound 2024-10-01.
2.3 Commercial property: replacement-cost coverage on Covered Premises
    contents up to 250,000.00.

3. Deductibles
3.1 General liability: 2,500.00 per claim.
3.2 Product Liability Rider: 5,000.00 per claim.
3.3 Commercial property: 1,000.00 per claim.

4. Premium and invoicing
4.1 Annual premium for the Policy Period is 11,400.00, inclusive of the
    Product Liability Rider.
4.2 Insurer invoices the premium as INV-NMI-2025-002, payable on Net-30
    terms from the Effective Date.
4.3 No installment schedule applies; the premium is due in full under
    Section 4.2.

5. Continuity
5.1 The Product Liability Rider carries forward from the prior policy
    period without a gap in coverage between 2025-06-30 and the Effective
    Date.
5.2 All other terms of the Policy originally dated 2022-07-01 remain in
    effect for the Policy Period, except as restated in this Renewal.

6. Order of precedence
6.1 This Renewal controls over the Policy where the two conflict for the
    Policy Period.
