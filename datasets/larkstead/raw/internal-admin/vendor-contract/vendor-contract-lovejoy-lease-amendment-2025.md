THIRD-YEAR RENT ESCALATION AND TERM AMENDMENT

This amendment ("Amendment") is made 2025-06-16 (the "Amendment Date")
between Larkstead Goods Company, LLC ("Tenant") and Lovejoy Property
Partners ("Landlord", VEN-08), and amends the commercial lease between the
parties dated 2022-06-15 (the "Lease") for the 2,140 sq ft office and
showroom space in NW Portland, Oregon. This is the third annual rent
escalation applied under the Lease, following the escalations applied in
2023 and 2024.

1. Definitions
1.1 "Prior Rent" means 4,588.57 per month, the monthly rent in effect
    under the Lease since the second annual escalation on 2024-06-15.
1.2 "Escalated Rent" means the monthly rent restated under Section 2 of
    this Amendment.
1.3 "Extended Term" means the period from the Amendment Date through
    2028-06-30, replacing the term end date under the Lease.

2. Rent escalation
2.1 Effective 2025-07-01, monthly rent under the Lease is restated from
    Prior Rent to Escalated Rent of 4,726.23, a 3% increase computed on
    Prior Rent.
2.2 Rent remains due on the 1st of each month per Section 4 of the Lease,
    unchanged by this Amendment.
2.3 The June 2025 rent invoice, already issued at Prior Rent before this
    Amendment was signed, is not adjusted; Escalated Rent applies starting
    with the invoice for July 2025.

3. Term
3.1 The Lease term is extended to run through the Extended Term.
3.2 All renewal notice provisions under the Lease apply to the Extended
    Term's end date as restated in Section 1.3.

4. Escalator continuation
4.1 The annual 3% escalator under the Lease continues to apply each June
    for the balance of the Extended Term unless the parties agree
    otherwise in writing.

5. Order of precedence
5.1 This Amendment controls over the Lease where the two conflict. All
    other terms of the Lease dated 2022-06-15 stand, including the
    payment terms of Net-30 on Landlord's invoices.
