Finance rule: Corporate card issuance and reconciliation
Effective: 2024-09-16
Supersedes: none
Owner: Ana Petrova   Approved by: Mara Voss

Rule
Cards are issued to named individuals only, never a shared or department card, and only to Mara Voss, Dmitri Okafor, Tom Aldridge, and Sofia Grieg as of this rule's effective date. Every charge needs a receipt uploaded to Ledgerly within 5 business days of the charge posting, and every charge needs a category code from the standing Ledgerly list: travel, meals and client entertainment, software and subscriptions, freight and logistics exceptions, or marketing and ad spend. A charge with no receipt by day 5 gets flagged on the cardholder's statement; a charge with no receipt by day 10 escalates to Mara directly.

Scope
Applies to the four active cards and any card issued after this date. It does not apply to the Shopstack, ParcelPoint, or Mailloft subscription charges that already draft on the standing accounts payable schedule, since those never touch a personal card.

Procedure
The cardholder codes and uploads the receipt themselves; nobody else files on their behalf. Charges under 250.00 need nothing further once the receipt and code are in. Charges from 250.00 to 999.99 get a spot-check from Ana against the category and the standing expense policy, after the fact. Charges of 1000.00 or more need Mara's approval before the card is used, not after, so a cardholder anticipating a charge that size requests sign-off first and cites the amount to the cent in the request. If the receipt window closes with nothing filed, then the charge sits flagged until the cardholder clears it, and a second miss inside the same quarter goes to Mara as a pattern, not a one-off.

Worked example
Sofia charges 180.00 on 12 Nov 2024 for a paid social boost, category marketing and ad spend. She uploads the receipt 14 Nov. Two days in, under 250.00, so nothing further happens. Tom charges 340.00 for a client dinner with the Harborview Architects contact on 3 Oct 2024, codes it meals and client entertainment, uploads the receipt the next morning. That lands in the spot-check band, and Ana confirms the code against the policy the following week.
