## Definition
**Operating:** In the sales-crm domain, pricing refers to the methods and structures used to calculate the monetary value assigned to goods or services sold, including considerations for total cost, discounts, and additional charges such as tax and freight.

## Why It Compounds
Understanding pricing is crucial as it not only affects the profitability of the sale but also influences customer perceptions and purchasing decisions. A well-structured pricing strategy can aid in gaining competitive advantage, managing customer expectations, and driving sales growth. Adjusting the pricing model can significantly impact the overall sales performance and customer satisfaction.
