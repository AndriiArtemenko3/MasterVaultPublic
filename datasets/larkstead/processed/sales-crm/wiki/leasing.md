## Definition
**Operating:** Leasing in the sales-crm domain refers to a business model where companies, such as ErgoNest, offer products for use without requiring an upfront capital investment from the customer. This option allows businesses to access necessary equipment while alleviating financial burdens.

## Main Insights
- ErgoNest has launched a per-seat leasing option aimed specifically at B2B accounts, targeting corporate clients looking for flexible financial solutions.
- By utilizing leasing, ErgoNest eliminates the need for a significant upfront investment from prospects, making their offerings more accessible.
- There remains an unclear understanding of the procedures or conditions surrounding the end-of-lease phase in ErgoNest's leasing program, which could impact customer satisfaction.
- Any issues arising from leased chairs, such as breakage, remain the responsibility of ErgoNest to address, thereby ensuring continued product integrity and service provision for the customer.

## Why It Compounds
The concept of leasing is essential because it presents businesses with the opportunity to enhance their operational flexibility and financial management. By removing large upfront costs, leasing can stimulate market engagement and enable companies to invest in other areas of growth. Understanding the leasing model can also lead to refinements in the product lifecycle management, as well as improvements in customer relationships through sustained support during the leasing period.
