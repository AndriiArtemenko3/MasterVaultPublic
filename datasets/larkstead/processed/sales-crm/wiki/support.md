## Definition
**Operating:** In the sales-CRM domain, support refers to the services provided to customers after they purchase a product, designed to ensure their ongoing satisfaction and problem resolution. This includes handling inquiries, troubleshooting, and resolving issues that arise with the products sold.

## Main Insights
- A prospect reported waiting six days to receive a response regarding a wobbling desk leg, indicating potential delays in support response times.
- Support ticket escalation for a broken gas lift took over two weeks, pointing to inefficiencies in the escalation process and a lack of timely interventions.
- The lack of parts has been identified as a significant obstacle to escalation processes for ErgoNest, suggesting inventory issues impact service quality.

## Why It Compounds
The effectiveness and efficiency of support services are crucial for maintaining customer satisfaction and loyalty, which translates into long-term sales success. Poor response times and unresolved issues can lead to negative customer experiences, potential loss of repeat business, and damage to the company's reputation. In a competitive market, the ability to provide prompt and effective support can differentiate one CRM provider from another, emphasizing the need for continuous improvement in support operations.
