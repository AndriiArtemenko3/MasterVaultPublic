## Definition  
**Operating:** In sales and customer relationship management (CRM), a pricing tier is a structured system that establishes discount rates based on the quantity of products purchased. The B2B volume pricing tier, for example, offers a 15% discount for orders consisting of 25 or more units, incentivizing customers to purchase in bulk.  

## Main Insights  
- The B2B volume pricing tier allows a 15% discount for orders of 25 or more units.  
- Discounts from the pricing tier apply even if orders have mixed products, as long as the total equals or exceeds 25 units.  

## Why It Compounds  
Understanding pricing tiers is crucial for businesses aiming to optimize their sales strategies and customer engagement. By effectively implementing a tiered pricing model, companies can drive higher order volumes, encourage bulk purchases, and improve customer loyalty. This not only enhances revenue streams but also strengthens customer relationships by providing them with tangible savings.
