## Definition
**Operating:** The warranty policy in the sales-crm domain stipulates a standard one-year warranty on all items, effective January 15, 2024, with specific exceptions such as the two-year warranty for Vireo desk lamp units affected by a firmware issue. 

## Main Insights
- The standard warranty duration for desks, chairs, and accessories is one year as of now.  
- Vireo desk lamp units affected by a firmware issue are covered by a specific two-year warranty.  
- A new one-year warranty policy for all items becomes effective on January 15, 2024.  

## Why It Compounds
The warranty policy is crucial for maintaining customer trust and satisfaction, as it outlines the protection offered to consumers against potential defects or issues with purchases. A clear and structured warranty can influence purchasing decisions, reinforcing the brand's image and ensuring after-sales support that can enhance customer loyalty. Moreover, variations in warranty periods draw attention to particular products that may have unique issues, helping customers make informed choices.
