## Definition

- Quotes are valid for a period of 30 days from the proposal date, encouraging timely decision-making by customers.
- The specific quote mentioned as valid dates from September 18, 2025, implying a strategic approach to managing sales timelines.
- Sales teams must ensure that proposals are followed up within the validity period to avoid quote expiration, which may lead to lost opportunities.

## Why It Compounds
Understanding quote validity is crucial for maintaining effective sales cycles and fostering strong customer relationships. When quotes expire, it not only reflects a lapse in initiative from the sales team but can also diminish customer trust and interest. The ability to navigate and communicate deadlines effectively can mean the difference between closing a deal or losing a client, making this concept foundational to successful sales strategies.
