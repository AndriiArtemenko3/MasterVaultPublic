## What's inside each bundle

Fledgling pairs the 48 in Birch desk with a Rowan chair in graphite black and a Sparrow cable kit. Canopy steps up to the 60 in Birch desk, adds a single Heron arm, and keeps the same chair and cable kit. Roost is the full setup: the 72 in Birch desk, the Rowan chair, a dual Heron arm, the Vireo lamp, a charcoal Alder mat, and the Magpie cable tray.

## Savings vs itemized

Buy the Fledgling pieces separately and the register rings up 973.00; the bundle price saves 74.00. Canopy's parts price out to 1,202.00 itemized against 1,099.00 bundled, a 103.00 gap. Roost is the biggest single saving on the sheet: 1,570.00 itemized versus 1,449.00 bundled, 121.00 back in the customer's pocket for buying the whole set instead of six separate line items.

## Notes

No volume tier applies here. These three bundle prices hold regardless of quantity; only itemized line-item orders qualify for the volume tier. Heavy freight still bills on every bundle order, no matter the size. Quotes built from this sheet hold for 30 days from issue.
