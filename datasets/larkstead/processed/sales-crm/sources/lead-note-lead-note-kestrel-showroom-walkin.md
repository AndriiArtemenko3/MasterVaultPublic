She's holding a per-seat lease offer from ErgoNest and wanted to know why buying beats leasing before she'd even sat in a chair.

Ten seats sits under our twenty-five unit tier so there's no discount to offer, quoted straight list. She didn't blink at the number, seemed more focused on the lease-versus-buy question than the price itself.

Next: send a written lease-vs-buy comparison plus a ten-seat quote by 2025-12-15. Owner: TA.
