## Question

Is B2B actually pulling its weight this quarter, and which segments are carrying the growth?

## Findings

1. Nine B2B orders shipped in Q3 against twenty-three DTC orders in the same window, a third of order volume out of a twelfth of the customer roster. Ana's rough pull on dollar mix puts B2B at 29% of quarter revenue, up from an estimated 21% the same quarter last year, and that's the number I've been waiting to report since before summer.
2. Petrel Analytics closed 2025-08-21 at 15 seats, drop-shipped to fifteen separate home addresses because the team works fully remote. One order per seat is a slower fulfillment shape than a single-site close, but it's the biggest single win logged this quarter, and Sanjay never once asked about a volume discount.
3. Healthcare is a segment nobody assigned me and I'm watching it anyway. Summit Physio Group keeps reordering Finch footrests like clockwork, and Bluebird Therapy Collective came in through a referral in September, Ruth Amadi still deciding on room counts before she commits. Two names in a segment that had exactly one a year ago counts as growing, even at n=2.
4. Juniper Cowork's 55 seats would be our second-largest account if it closes. Dana Whitcombe wants staggered invoicing per floor across three floors, which is a new structure for us, and legal hasn't weighed in on whether our template supports it yet.
5. Foxglove isn't new pipeline. It's an existing 26-seat account with an ErgoNest quote on the table and a renewal review that isn't scheduled yet. No number to report, just a flag to keep it on next quarter's QBR too.

## Caveats

n=12 B2B accounts, four still open. The 29% revenue-share figure comes from Ana's average-order-value estimate, not a closed Ledgerly period, so treat it as directional and not a number to put in front of Mara without a footnote. Petrel's fifteen one-seat drop-ships also mean per-account order counts aren't comparable across the rest of the roster this quarter.

Next: get Juniper's staggered-invoicing ask in front of Colefield & Tran for a template check, by 2025-10-24. Owner: TA.
