## Content

Opportunity: PW-foxglove-winback
Account: Foxglove Studios
Logged: 2025-11-12 by TA
Source: call 2025-11-12

Objection (their words): "ErgoNest lands in two days anywhere out west. You're telling me three to five. On a shoot schedule that gap costs us money every single time a set isn't ready."
Category: competitor

Response given: Tom didn't argue the transit numbers, since Birch frames ship freight as a heavy item and a light parcel from a rival's stock will always beat that on paper, seat for seat. He offered staged delivery instead: split Foxglove's order so the desks and chairs Mia needs for the first build days arrive ahead of monitor arms and lamps, which can trail a few days without stalling a shoot. That part landed. He also flagged the 45-day return window, in force since 2025-11-03, as a backstop if a piece misses her calendar.
Evidence used: 45-day return window effective 2025-11-03; Birch desk frames ship as heavy freight, unlike ErgoNest's parcel-only stock; Foxglove's 26-seat renewal decision due 2026-08.
Landed: partly. Mia agreed staging could work, didn't drop the shipping-speed complaint.

Next: draft a staged-delivery schedule mapped to Foxglove's shoot calendar by 2025-11-21. Owner: TA.
