## Scope

15 Fledgling workstation bundles for Petrel's fully remote team, one per new hire, shipped to individual home addresses instead of a single dock. Sanjay Iyer wants every hire on the same configuration so nobody ends up with a lesser setup than a teammate. Two of the fifteen roles are still being backfilled, so this quote covers the full seat count now while the remaining home addresses get collected and confirmed individually over the next several weeks as Sanjay's hiring finishes. Each bundle is the same 48 in desk, graphite chair, and cable kit regardless of which address it ships to, and each unit's height-adjustment memory is set to a standard preset before it leaves the warehouse.

| LS-BDL-001 | Fledgling workstation bundle | 15 | 899.00 | 13,485.00 |

Discount: none (below the 25-unit tier, effective 2024-06-01)
Subtotal: 13,485.00
Sales tax: assessed per delivery address at invoicing, per the tax table, since addresses span more than one state
Freight: drop-ship, billed per address at the standard heavy-item zone rate, quoted individually as each address is confirmed
Total: 13,485.00 before tax and freight, both determined per address

## Terms

Payment net 30 from each unit's ship date, invoiced per address rather than as a single order. No discount applies. Delivery staged as addresses are confirmed, with the two open roles' units following once those hires start. Petrel will receive one combined invoice for the first wave of confirmed addresses, with the remaining two units invoiced separately once those hires are in place. 1-year warranty on all items (warranty policy effective 2024-01-15). Returns per the 30-day window (effective 2024-01-15); 10% restocking fee is waived on this order under the B2B waiver for orders of 10 or more units (effective 2025-06-02). Quote valid 30 days from 2025-07-09.
