## Scope

Six Heron dual monitor arms for the shared review stations in Harborview's downstairs model shop, verbally agreed as part of the 22-seat order Elena Brandt signed 2024-09-19. Elena's finance team asked for the arms broken onto their own PO for capital-equipment tracking, separate from the Canopy bundle invoice covering the 22 upstairs seats. Same account, new paperwork. The model-shop stations aren't tied to one staffer; whoever is reviewing drawings that day sits there, which is why they carry the dual arm instead of the single arm that already ships in each seat's bundle. The dual arm clamps onto the same desk edge as the single-arm version, so no additional drilling or new desk hardware is needed at install, and the swap can happen room by room without shutting the model shop down for a day. Ana Petrova will cut this as its own invoice once the signed PO comes back from Elena's team.
