Note by: TA, 2026-01-08

Marcus called about outfitting the whole firm, 18 seats, one floor on Broadway. Straightforward on the surface, except he stopped me halfway through the pitch to say nothing gets signed until firm counsel has redlined whatever paperwork we send over, which for a law firm tracks, they probably redline their own takeout orders. Quoted the Canopy bundle at list since 18 sits well under any tier threshold. No discount to offer. He didn't push on price at all, just wanted the contract language in front of his partners before anyone looks at chairs. Asked what a normal MSA turnaround looks like on our side, told him two weeks once we send a draft, faster if his counsel doesn't ask for much. Fair enough, he said, and set a follow-up for later in the month.

Next: send draft MSA to Marcus for firm counsel review by 2026-01-22. Owner: TA.
