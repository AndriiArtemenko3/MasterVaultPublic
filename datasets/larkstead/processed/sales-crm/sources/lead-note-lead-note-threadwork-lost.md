Opportunity: PW-threadwork-12seat

Contact: Derek Osei (office manager)

Est. value: 10,788.00 list (12x LS-BDL-001 at 899.00), lost
Note by: TA, 2025-07-14

Small Beaverton startup, twelve seats, Derek's the office manager and the one signing off on furniture. Quoted the twelve-seat number a few weeks back, followed up today and he'd already gone another way.

They bought a pallet of secondhand desks and chairs off a liquidated tech office, said it worked out to under half our number. Didn't push back hard on quality, just straight cost, twelve seats is a real chunk of a seed-stage budget and he said as much more than once.

Asked if there's a rebuy horizon. He mentioned they're raising a Series A sometime in 2026 and furniture is not on anyone's mind until that closes. Marking closed-lost for now rather than leaving it open and stale.

Next: check back with Derek Osei once Threadwork announces a Series A, no earlier than 2026-04-01. Owner: TA.
