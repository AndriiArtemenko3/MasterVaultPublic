He offered staggered invoicing tied to each floor going live, so Dana isn't billed for floor three before it exists. She pushed back anyway. He reminded her the 15% B2B tier already applies at her seat count, no extra ask needed.
Evidence used: LS-BDL-002 at 1,099.00 per seat, 15% tier effective 2024-06-01 (applies at 25+ seats), phased-floor invoicing per PW-juniper-cowork-55seat notes.
Landed: partly. Dana liked the staggered billing, still wants a lease comparison in writing before floor two.
