## Scope

Nine seats for Bluebird's Portland practice: two front-desk seats and seven practitioners who rotate between charting and treatment through the day, which is why this quote covers Rowan task chairs on their own rather than a full desk-and-chair bundle, since Ruth confirmed her treatment rooms already run on massage tables and floor mats and don't need a desk added to them. Standing desks came up on the December call with Yuki, but Ruth walked the rooms again since then and decided the practice doesn't need them yet. Eighteen Finch footrests replace the six quoted back in December, two per treatment room across all nine rooms, since clients need their own during intake instead of sharing whatever the practitioner isn't using that hour. Single wave. No staggering by room.
