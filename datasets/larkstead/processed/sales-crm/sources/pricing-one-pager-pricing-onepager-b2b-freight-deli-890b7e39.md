## Delivery options

Palletized LTL is the default for any order landing at one address: desks, chairs, arms, and bundles ship together on a pallet out of the Reno dock, and the freight line on the invoice matches whatever the carrier actually charged for that load, nothing added on top. Showroom pickup is free with no minimum order, available at the Portland location during business hours; will-call orders not collected within 10 business days of the ready date convert automatically to standard freight. Drop-ship parcel service exists for remote or multi-site teams: each seat ships as its own parcel straight to the employee's address instead of one pallet to a single site, billed at the zone rate below for that item's weight class.

## Notes

The 75.00 free-[[free-shipping-threshold]] waives light and medium rates only; heavy items bill the zone rate on every order, drop-shipped or not. Delivery is not a discount. Palletized freight, showroom pickup, and drop-ship are three ways to route product, not three price breaks, and none of them touches the volume tier on the pricing sheet. Quotes hold for 30 days from issue.
