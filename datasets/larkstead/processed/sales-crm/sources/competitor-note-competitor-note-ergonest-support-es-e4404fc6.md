## Content

Competitor: ErgoNest
Card revised: 2025-04-09 by TA
Prior revision: 2024-11-06
Sources: a discovery call on 2025-03-18; a secondhand account relayed by a prospect's colleague on 2025-04-02; ergonest site checked 2025-04-07.

## Positioning

Direct-import reseller of white-label ergonomic desks and chairs, sells on price and shipping speed. Support keeps surfacing as the soft spot in that pitch. Two more data points this cycle.

## Price posture

No change to log. Still the 10-18% band with weekly codes.

## Where they win

- lower sticker, and 2-day shipping still holds up as a real edge on the desk side
- 9 chair models to our 1, unchanged since November

## Where we win

- in-house support. The March 18 prospect said she opened a ticket about a wobbling desk leg and didn't hear back for six days, then got routed to a different rep who asked her to explain the whole issue over again from the start
- the April 2 account came secondhand, from a colleague of a prospect who'd bought an ErgoNest chair last year. Escalation on a broken gas lift reportedly took over two weeks and ended in a partial credit instead of a replacement part, because there wasn't one to send
- the parts story and the support story compound each other. No part means an escalation has nowhere fast to go

## Ground rules

Two accounts, not a verified pattern. Hold that distinction when repeating this internally. Nothing customer-facing names them.
