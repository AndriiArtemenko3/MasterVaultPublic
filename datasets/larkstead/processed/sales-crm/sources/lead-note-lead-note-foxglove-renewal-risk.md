I spoke with Mia Serrano today regarding the studio's refresh decision. She confirmed she is holding a written quote from ErgoNest that runs approximately fourteen percent under our current pricing for a comparable twenty-six seat configuration, and she stated plainly that the decision will not be made before August of 2026.

Mia raised no complaint about [[product-quality]] or service during the call. The stated driver is price alone. I asked whether ErgoNest's fourteen-day return window or lack of a replacement parts catalog had come up in her evaluation, and she said it had not been discussed yet with her team.

I intend to prepare a written comparison covering total cost of ownership, including parts availability and our replacement warranty terms, well ahead of the August deadline rather than waiting until the final weeks.

Next: deliver the total cost of ownership comparison to Mia Serrano by 2026-03-02. Owner: YT.
