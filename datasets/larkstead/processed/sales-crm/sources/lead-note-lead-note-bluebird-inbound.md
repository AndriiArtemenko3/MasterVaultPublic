She took it well, said the parts catalog mattered more to her than a percentage off anyway, something about how often chair casters get replaced in a clinic.

Quoted at list, 4,467.00, no negotiation expected on this one.

Next: send the written quote and confirm final footrest count by 2025-10-17. Owner: TA.
