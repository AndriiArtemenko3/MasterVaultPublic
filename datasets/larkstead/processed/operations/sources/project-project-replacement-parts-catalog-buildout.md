## Content

Project: Replacement-parts catalog buildout, wave 3
Owner: Dmitri Okafor (DO)
Opened: 2024-06-10   Target close: 2024-08-19
Related: VEN-07, VEN-02, LS-ACC-010, LS-ACC-011

Goal
Add two standalone replacement-part SKUs, the Birch motor control box
(LS-ACC-010) and the Birch leveling feet set (LS-ACC-011), live on Shopstack
by 2024-08-19, with 300 and 500 units respectively landed at the Portland
backroom parts shelf ahead of launch.

Plan
- Helprise ticket pull covering the prior two quarters flags 61 requests for
  a standalone motor control box and 44 for leveling feet, both well past
  the threshold Dmitri uses to decide a part earns its own SKU rather than
  staying a warranty-only replacement.
- Motor control box sourced from Meridian Components (VEN-07), the same
  vendor already supplying Sparrow cable kits and power adapters. Quote
  comes back at 34.00 landed per unit, 300-unit minimum order, lot
  LOT-2024-19.
- Leveling feet set sourced from Ostrava Metalworks (VEN-02), same vendor as
  the desk frames the feet actually bolt onto. Quote at 3.00 landed per
  unit, 500-unit minimum, lot LOT-2024-20.
- Order cost: 300 units at 34.00 is 10,200.00 for the motor box run; 500
  units at 3.00 is 1,500.00 for the feet run.
- Both parts fit all three Birch desk widths, 48, 60, and 72 in, so one
  listing each rather than splitting by desk size.
- GreenCrate (VEN-05) mailers sized to the smaller of the two parts, since
  neither ships in anything close to a desk carton.
- Priya's team adds a warranty-check note to both listings: desks still
  inside the 1 year standard warranty get the part free through a ticket,
  not a paid Shopstack order.
- Pricing set at 89.00 for the motor box and 15.00 for the feet set, both
  priced to clear a healthy margin over landed cost without looking like a
  markup on a part that used to ship free under warranty.

Risks
- Meridian's 300-unit run slips past the 05 Aug receiving date: fallback
  launches the leveling feet SKU on schedule alone and holds the motor box
  listing until stock actually lands, rather than selling against a
  promise.
- Warranty overlap confuses customers who are still covered: fallback is
  the Priya team's listing note, reviewed before either SKU goes live, not
  bolted on after the first ticket comes in.
- Ostrava substitutes a feet-set finish that doesn't match the Birch desk
  leg color: fallback is Hank's team holding the lot at receiving for a
  side-by-side check against a desk sample already on the backroom floor.

Log
10 Jun: kickoff. Helprise pull confirms 61 and 44 requests, both cleared for
a standalone SKU.
24 Jun: Meridian quote back, 34.00 landed, 300-unit minimum, LOT-2024-19.
08 Jul: Ostrava quote back, 3.00 landed, 500-unit minimum, LOT-2024-20.
22 Jul: both orders placed. GreenCrate mailer samples requested same week.
05 Aug: LOT-2024-19 and LOT-2024-20 land at the Portland backroom, 300 and
500 units. Hank spot-checks 20 of each against the desk sample on the floor,
clean.
19 Aug: both SKUs live on Shopstack, 89.00 and 15.00, warranty note attached
to each listing.
