## Content

Project: Cobalt Dental bulk fulfillment, order #LS31695
Owner: Dmitri Okafor (DO)
Opened: 2025-11-21   Target close: 2026-02-18
Related: PW-cobalt-dental-40seat, VEN-04, VEN-02, VEN-07, LOT-2025-31, LOT-2025-44, LOT-2025-46

Goal
Deliver 40 LS-BDL-002 Canopy bundle kits plus 2 LS-LMP-001 lamps across four clinics, one wave per clinic, zero missed wave dates, last wave landing 2026-02-18.

Plan
- 40 bundle kits assembled at ParcelPoint (VEN-04) Reno. Kitting fee 6.00 per kit, 40 kits, 240.00 total.
- Desk frames pulled from LOT-2025-44, chair bases from LOT-2025-46, both Ostrava Metalworks (VEN-02). Frame and base counts checked against the order before kitting starts, not after.
- 2 Vireo lamps ship with wave 1, sourced from Meridian Components (VEN-07) PCB rev C stock, LOT-2025-31.
- Four waves, 10 seats per wave: clinic 1 on 2025-12-10, clinic 2 on 2026-01-07, clinic 3 on 2026-01-28, clinic 4 on 2026-02-18.
- Load: 12 pallets total, 122 cartons total, 3 pallets per wave, palletized LTL at carrier cost, billed per clinic.
- Sam Ortiz gets a dock-day confirmation call from Yuki 3 business days ahead of each wave, not from me; she owns the account now.

Risks
- Kitting slot slippage at ParcelPoint over the December holiday schedule: fallback is splitting wave 1 kitting across two dock days, 20 kits each.
- LOT-2025-31 PCB rev C interacts with firmware v1.2 sleep timing on some units: fallback is Yuki routes any flicker complaints straight to me, no separate warranty process yet.
- Clinic 3's building shares one freight elevator with two other tenants: fallback is booking the dock slot two days ahead through the property manager.
- Steel surcharge chatter out of Ostrava could push frame costs before wave 4: fallback is locking wave 4 frame stock at Reno by January, ahead of any 2026 price move.

Log
21 Nov: kitting slots booked, 40 kits across four batches, one per wave.
21 Nov: LOT-2025-31 lamp stock confirmed on hand at Reno, no reorder needed before wave 1.
21 Nov: confirmed with Ostrava that LOT-2025-44 and LOT-2025-46 both clear customs before the wave 1 kitting date.
