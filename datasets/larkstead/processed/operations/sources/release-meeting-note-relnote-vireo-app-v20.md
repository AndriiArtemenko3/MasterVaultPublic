## Content

Release notes: Vireo companion app v2.0
Released: 2026-05-06
Publisher: Bitgrove Labs (VEN-03) app team; posted by Dmitri Okafor
Applies to: the Vireo phone app; no hardware changes, all LS-LMP-001 units

Fixed
- Scheduling screen sometimes showed a schedule as active on the wrong day after a phone's time zone changed mid-trip. Schedule storage now keys off UTC instead of local time.

Changed
- Scheduling screen redesigned: sleep schedule and timed scene changes sit on one screen instead of two, plus a one-tap skip-tonight button.
- Each connected unit's card now shows its firmware version directly, so a customer or an agent can see fw-v1.0 through fw-v1.3 without opening settings three levels deep.

Known issues
- None open at release.

Rollout
App store release, available to all users on update. No firmware ships with this release.

Support
When a ticket needs a firmware number, point the customer to the device card in the redesigned app instead of the old settings path.
