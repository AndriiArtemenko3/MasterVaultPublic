## Definition
**Operating:** As of January 15, 2026, orders of $99.00 or more in merchandise value will qualify for free shipping. This new free shipping threshold replaces the previous threshold of $75.00 for orders placed on or after the effective date.

## Main Insights
- The free shipping threshold increases from $75.00 to $99.00 effective January 15, 2026.
- Orders placed before the effective date, January 15, 2026, will still honor the former threshold of $75.00.
- The new threshold applies only to orders made on or after the effective date of January 15, 2026.

## Why It Compounds
The implementation of a free shipping threshold significantly impacts customer purchasing behavior, incentivizing customers to adjust their shopping cart totals to meet the new limit of $99.00. This policy not only facilitates higher sales volumes for businesses but can also enhance customer satisfaction as they feel encouraged to add more items to their carts to benefit from free shipping. By understanding these dynamics, companies can strategically enhance their marketing efforts and optimize inventory turnover.
