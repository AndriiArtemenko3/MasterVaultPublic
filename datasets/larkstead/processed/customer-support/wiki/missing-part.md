## Definition
**Operating:** In customer support, a "Missing Part" refers to a situation where a component or accessory that is included in an order is not present upon delivery. This occurrence highlights the importance of thorough order fulfillment and quality control measures in ensuring customer satisfaction.

## Main Insights
- The specific case involving the sparrow cable kit order #LS20705 indicates that the adhesive clips were not included in the shipment.
- As a resolution, a replacement pack of adhesive clips is being sent to the customer, Derek, at no additional cost.

## Why It Compounds
The issue of missing parts in customer orders can lead to significant dissatisfaction and inconvenience for the customer, potentially impacting brand loyalty and future purchasing decisions. If customers frequently experience missing items, it can signal larger operational inefficiencies within logistics and fulfillment processes that need to be addressed for overall improvement in customer service.
