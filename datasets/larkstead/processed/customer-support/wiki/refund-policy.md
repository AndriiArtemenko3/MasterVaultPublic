## Definition
**Operating:** The current refund policy allows customers to return items within a specified period for a refund under certain conditions. Unopened items in their original packaging can be returned for a full refund, while opened items that are non-defective incur a 10% restocking fee. Defective items can be returned for a full refund with no fees. 

## Main Insights
- Customers have a 30-day window from delivery to request a full refund if the item is unused and in its original packaging.
- Opened, non-defective returns incur a 10% restocking fee, resulting in a refund of 90% of the purchase price.
- Full refunds are processed back to the original payment methods within 5 business days of the warehouse confirming the return.
- Defective items qualify for a full refund without any restocking fees.

## Why It Compounds
Understanding refund policies is critical for both customers and businesses as it establishes trust and ensures satisfaction. A transparent and fair refund policy can enhance customer loyalty and trust, encouraging repeat purchases. For businesses, a well-defined policy minimizes confusion and helps manage returns effectively, maintaining financial integrity while also ensuring customer happiness.
