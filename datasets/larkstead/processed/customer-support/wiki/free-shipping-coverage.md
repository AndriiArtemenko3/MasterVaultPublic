## Definition
**Operating:** Free Shipping Coverage is a shipping policy that waives shipping fees for light and medium items, making it an attractive option for customers who want to avoid additional costs on smaller purchases. However, this coverage does not extend to heavy items, which continue to incur standard shipping rates.

## Main Insights
- Free shipping applies only to light and medium items, thereby providing savings on smaller purchases for customers.
- Heavy items are excluded from the free shipping policy, meaning they will still incur shipping costs regardless of the order's total value.
- The policy is likely to encourage customers to purchase more light and medium items to take advantage of the shipping savings.

## Why It Compounds
Free Shipping Coverage plays an important role in customer experience and retention by reducing barriers to purchasing. By offering to waive shipping fees on lighter items, retailers can enhance customer satisfaction and encourage higher purchase volumes. This practice not only boosts immediate sales but also fosters long-term loyalty, as customers feel they are getting added value with their purchases, even if not all items are covered under this policy.
