## Definition
**Operating:** The current shipping policy states that orders above a certain threshold are eligible for free shipping to enhance customer satisfaction and encourage larger purchases. 

## Main Insights
- The help page incorrectly mentions that orders over $75.00 qualify for free shipping, which may mislead customers regarding current promotions.
- The help center has not been updated to reflect the newly established shipping threshold, potentially causing further confusion among customers.
- Accurate shipping policy information is essential for ensuring transparent customer communication and maintaining trust. 

## Why It Compounds
Shipping policy is critical in the customer support domain as it directly affects customer expectations, purchasing decisions, and overall satisfaction. An unclear or outdated shipping policy can lead to misunderstandings, dissatisfied customers, and lost sales. Ensuring that customers have access to the correct information fosters a positive shopping experience and enhances brand loyalty, making it crucial for businesses to regularly update their policies and customer support resources.
