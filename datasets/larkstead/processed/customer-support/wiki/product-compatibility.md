## Definition
**Operating:** Product compatibility refers to the ability of various devices or equipment to function seamlessly with one another. In the customer-support domain, this often focuses on how well a product, such as a laptop stand, can accommodate different laptop sizes. 

## Main Insights
- The Wren stand is designed to securely hold devices ranging from 10-inch tablet-laptop hybrids up to 17-inch gaming laptops.
- Laptops that exceed approximately 15.5 inches in width will have a slight overhang on the sides when placed on the Wren stand.

## Why It Compounds
Understanding product compatibility is vital for both customers and manufacturers, as it directly impacts user satisfaction and product functionality. When a product is marketed, the specifications regarding compatibility increase consumer trust, ensuring that they can make informed decisions about their purchases. A clear understanding of compatibility helps in avoiding returns or dissatisfaction due to misfit devices, which can enhance overall sales and brand reputation.
