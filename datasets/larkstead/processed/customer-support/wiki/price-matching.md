## Definition
**Operating:** Larkstead does not price match against any competitor, retailer, or marketplace listing, but it will match the list price of an identical-spec item sold by a US-based seller within 14 days of purchase. This policy allows customers to potentially benefit from lower prices in a limited timeframe while ensuring consistent pricing strategies across the board.  

## Main Insights
- Larkstead only matches list prices, which means any discounts, promotions, or bundled offers from competitors are not considered.  
- The price match guarantee is applicable only if the identical product is purchased from a US-based seller.
- This price adjustment can be requested within a maximum window of 14 days after the initial purchase.
- Price matching does not apply retroactively after the 14-day period has elapsed.  

## Why It Compounds  
Understanding price matching is essential for both businesses and customers as it builds trust and assurance regarding pricing fairness. When customers are confident that they can get the best price available, they are more likely to complete their purchases. Moreover, price matching can play a role in competitive positioning within retail markets, influencing customer loyalty and shopping behavior.
