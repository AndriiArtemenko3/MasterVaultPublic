## Definition
**Operating:** The current operating practice involves addressing customer concerns regarding the flickering of Vireo lamps, especially when dimmed below 20% brightness at night. This flickering issue has been reported by various customers and is recognized as a recurring support challenge.

The Vireo flicker issue is significant beyond individual reports due to its implications for customer satisfaction and product reliability. Understanding and addressing this concern swiftly not only helps improve user experience but also protects the company's reputation in the competitive lighting market.
