## Definition
**Operating:** Free shipping is a promotional shipping method whereby customers do not incur shipping fees on their orders when they meet specific criteria. Typically, this practice applies to orders totaling $75.00 or more and is limited to light and medium-sized items only.

## Main Insights
- Orders must reach a minimum total of $75.00 to qualify for free shipping.
- The free shipping offer is restricted to light and medium items, meaning larger or heavier products may still incur shipping fees.
- This practice is a common marketing strategy to encourage larger purchases and improve customer satisfaction.

## Why It Compounds
Free shipping is an essential aspect of customer support and service as it not only enhances the shopping experience but also incentivizes customers to increase their order total, ultimately driving sales. By setting a threshold for free shipping, businesses can encourage higher cart values while effectively managing shipping costs. This strategy strengthens customer loyalty and can lead to repeat purchases, making it a valuable tool in a competitive marketplace.
