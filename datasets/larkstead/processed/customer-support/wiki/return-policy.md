## Definition
**Operating:** As of January 12, 2026, customers may return any item within 45 days of the delivery date, which is a permanent policy. This allows customers to have a reasonable window to assess the quality and functionality of products purchased, thus enhancing customer satisfaction and trust in the brand.

## Main Insights
- The 45-day return window applies to every order year-round, ensuring consistency across all purchases.
- Specific items, such as the Vireo lamp ordered on November 3, 2025, fall under this 45-day return policy.
- Returns for opened non-defective items, like mats, incur a restocking fee of 10%.
- The return window for the stand and Larkstead orders is set to 30 days effective from January 15, 2024, highlighting variability in return policies for different items.  

## Why It Compounds
The return policy is critical for customer support as it directly influences customer experience and satisfaction. A flexible return policy enhances consumer confidence, encouraging them to make purchases without the fear of being stuck with unsatisfactory products. It also aids in building long-term loyalty and a positive brand image, as customers feel valued and supported in their purchasing decisions. Furthermore, clear policies regarding defective products, such as the option to recycle rather than return, promote sustainable practices and customer goodwill.
