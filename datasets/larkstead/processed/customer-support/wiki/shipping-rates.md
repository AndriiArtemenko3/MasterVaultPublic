## Definition
**Operating:** Shipping rates are the charges applied to deliver goods from a seller to a buyer, based on specific criteria such as item weight and delivery zone. These rates fluctuate based on predefined zones and whether the customer qualifies for free shipping.

## Main Insights
- Heavy items always bill the applicable zone rate regardless of order total or free-shipping eligibility.
- Zone rates have not changed as a result of recent updates.
- In Zone US-1, light items are billed at $5.95, medium items at $12.95, and heavy items at $49.00 when free shipping is not available.

## Why It Compounds
Understanding shipping rates is crucial for businesses and consumers alike, as it influences purchasing decisions and overall customer satisfaction. Transparent shipping costs can enhance customer trust and reduce abandoned carts, while unclear or unexpectedly high rates may deter potential buyers. Thus, having a clear structure for shipping rates not only optimizes operational costs but also improves the customer experience.
