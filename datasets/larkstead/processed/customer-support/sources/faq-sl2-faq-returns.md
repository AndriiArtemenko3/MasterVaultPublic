## Starting a return

Q: How long do I have to return something?
A: You have 30 days from delivery to start a return. We go by the
delivery date ParcelPoint has on file, not the day you placed the
order.

Q: How do I start one?
A: Email support@larkstead.example with your #LS order number and tell
us what you're sending back. We'll reply with instructions for getting
it to the warehouse.

Q: Do I need the original box?
A: It helps, but it's not required. If you don't have it anymore, just
pack the item so it won't shift around in transit.

## Fees and condition

Q: Will I get charged a fee?
A: Only if the item has been opened and it isn't defective. Those
returns carry a 10% restocking fee, taken out of your refund. Unopened
items in original packaging get a full refund, no fee at all.

Q: What if the item is defective?
A: No fee, ever. Tell us what's wrong when you email us and we'll sort
out a replacement or a refund, whichever you'd rather have.

## Getting your money back

Q: When do I get my refund?
A: Refunds land within 5 business days after the warehouse checks the
item in. It goes back to whatever card or account you paid with
originally.

Q: Can I get store credit instead?
A: Not right now. Refunds only go back to the original payment method,
so plan on that rather than a credit toward a future order.
