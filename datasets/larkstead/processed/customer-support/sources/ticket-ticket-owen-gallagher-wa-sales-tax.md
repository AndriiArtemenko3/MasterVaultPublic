--- Internal note (CM) --- 2025-07-22 11:05 PT
order #LS60113, confirmed first. LS-STD-001, 79.00, shipping to Vancouver WA not Portland OR. tax is based on delivery address not our HQ, WA rate is 6.5%. 79.00 x 0.065 = 5.135, rounds to 5.14, matches what he saw. explaining the destination-based rule, not a hidden fee.

--- Message 2 (agent, public reply) --- 2025-07-22 11:30 PT
Hi Owen, can you confirm the order number for me first, just so I'm looking at the right one? I see #LS60113, the Wren stand at 79.00 shipping to a Vancouver, WA address. Sales tax is calculated on where an order ships to, not where we're based, and Washington's rate is 6.5%, which comes out to 5.14 on a 79.00 order. Oregon has no sales tax, which is true for orders shipping inside Oregon, but Vancouver is just across the river in Washington so it falls under that state's rate instead. Nothing hidden here, and it's not something we can waive since it's collected on the state's behalf.

Celeste
Larkstead Support
