## About the lamp

Q: What is the Vireo lamp and how much does it cost?
A: The Vireo smart desk lamp (LS-LMP-001) is our connected task light with app-controlled brightness, color temperature, and a sleep-mode dimming schedule. It's 149.00 and ships from the Portland warehouse within two business days of ordering.

Q: Do I need an account to use it?
A: You need the Vireo app to pair the lamp over Wi-Fi, but no account is required for basic on/off and brightness control from the physical base dial.

## Firmware & updates

Q: How does the lamp get firmware updates?
A: Updates arrive over the air. Once the lamp is connected to your home Wi-Fi, it checks for new firmware automatically in the background, usually overnight, and the app will show a small update badge when one installs. You don't need to do anything for a normal update.

Q: Can I turn off automatic updates?
A: Not right now. We push firmware changes to every connected unit as part of keeping the lamp secure and reliable, so there's no per-user opt-out yet.

## Warranty & returns

Q: What's the warranty on the Vireo lamp?
A: One year from delivery. If a defect shows up in normal use during that window, we'll repair or replace the unit at no cost.

Q: I want to return mine. What are the rules?
A: You have 30 days from delivery for a full refund if the lamp is unused and in its original packaging. Opened returns that aren't defective carry a 10 percent restocking fee, which comes out of the refund automatically, so a customer returning an opened, working lamp gets 90 percent of the purchase price back.

Q: What if the lamp arrives damaged or doesn't power on?
A: That's a defective return, no restocking fee. Contact support first so we can confirm it's a hardware issue rather than a setup problem, and we'll send a prepaid return label.

## Getting help

Q: Something's wrong with my lamp. What do I do?
A: Reach out through our helpdesk with your #LS order number handy; that's the fastest way for the team to pull up your order and firmware history. Include a short description of what you're seeing and, if it's a display or light behavior, a quick video helps a lot.

Q: How fast will someone respond?
A: Support runs Monday through Friday and typically replies within one business day. If your issue involves the lamp not powering on at all, flag that in the subject line so it gets triaged faster.

Q: Where do I check my order number if I don't have the confirmation email?
A: Log into your account on the storefront and look under Order history, or search your inbox for "Larkstead order confirmation." The number always starts with #LS.
