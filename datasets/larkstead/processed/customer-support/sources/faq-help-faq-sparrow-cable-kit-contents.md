## What comes in the box

Q: What exactly do I get with the Sparrow cable kit?
A: Each LS-ACC-002 kit has 8 adhesive-back cable clips, two 12 inch adhesive cord channels, 20 reusable hook-and-loop ties, and 4 alcohol prep pads. It's built as a starter kit for one desk, not a bulk pack, so if you're wiring up a monitor arm, a dock, and a lamp on the same desktop, one kit is usually tight but workable.

Q: Do I need my own tools to use it?
A: No. Everything peels, sticks, or wraps by hand.

## Prepping the surface before you stick anything down

Q: How do I get a clip to actually stay stuck?
A: Wipe the spot with one of the included alcohol pads first, then let it air dry for about a minute before pressing the clip down, because any dust, oil from your hands, or leftover cleaner residue is the single most common reason a clip lets go within the first week. Press firmly along the whole footprint of the clip for a few seconds rather than just tapping the center, and if you can, avoid loading it with cable weight for the first hour so the bond has time to set.

Q: Will the adhesive hurt my desktop finish or the frame paint?
A: On a Birch desktop or the powder-coated frame, no, the clips remove cleanly if you ever need to reposition one. On unfinished wood, painted drywall, or anything you've refinished yourself, test one clip in an inconspicuous spot first.

Q: A clip fell off after a week. What went wrong?
A: Almost always surface prep, cold or humid rooms slow the cure, or the run of cable clipped in was too heavy for a single clip to hold. Re-wipe the spot, let it dry longer this time, and split heavier cable runs across two clips instead of one.

## Running low on adhesive pads or clips

Q: Can I buy just a refill pack of clips or prep pads?
A: Not right now, the kit sells as one unit at $35.00 and we don't split it into refill packs. If you're only short a clip or two, a second full kit covers most reorders and the extra pieces are handy for a second cable run later.
