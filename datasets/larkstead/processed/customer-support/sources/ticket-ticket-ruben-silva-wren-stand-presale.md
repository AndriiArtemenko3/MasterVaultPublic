--- Message 2 (agent, public reply) --- 2024-04-19 11:52 PT
Hi, good question to ask before ordering. The Wren stand is rated to hold up
to 22 lbs, so a 6.5 lb laptop is well within what it's built for, no tipping
or flexing at that weight. The base is wide enough for most 17 in laptops
front to back too. The one thing worth checking on your end is the depth of
your specific model against the stand's platform, since a handful of the
largest 17 in gaming laptops run a little deeper than the standard frame. If
you can share the dimensions I'm happy to double check the fit before you
order.

Priya
Larkstead Support
