# Kb Birch Desk Wont Rise

## Symptom

The paddle switch does nothing at all, or the desk starts to rise and one leg visibly leads the other until the frame racks and stops mid-travel with the little display blinking. A few customers describe a clicking sound with no movement at all.

## Before you start

Have the order number ready so we can check purchase date against the warranty window. Ask whether the display panel is blank or blinking, and if the desk moves at all, which side leads: left or right.

## Steps

1. Unplug the control box at the wall for a full 30 seconds. Switching the paddle off is not the same thing and won't clear a racking fault.
2. Plug it back in and wait for the display to show a flashing dash before touching anything. If nothing lights up at all, stop here: the control box itself has likely failed and this reset won't help.
3. Hold the down arrow until the desk reaches its lowest travel and stops on its own, even if it grinds a little on the last inch. That grinding at the very bottom is normal during a reset and isn't a fault.
4. With the desk fully lowered, hold the up and down buttons together for 5 seconds until the display flashes twice. This re-syncs the leg-position memory so both sides read the same height internally, which is the actual fix for racking.
5. Run the desk to full height with the up arrow, watching both legs the whole way. Expected result: the two legs stay within about a quarter inch of each other from bottom to top.
6. If it racks again during step 5, stop there and move to the escalation section below rather than repeating the reset a second or third time.

## If the steps do not fix it

Check the purchase date against the standard 1-year warranty, effective 2024-01-15. Inside that window, Larkstead ships a replacement control box at no charge rather than billing for the part. Outside the window, the customer pays list for LS-ACC-010 (89.00) plus the shipping rate for their zone, and the swap is a straightforward unplug-and-reconnect job that doesn't need a technician visit. One shortcut worth remembering: a control box with no display at all after step 2 has already failed outright, so skip the rest of the reset and go straight to ordering the replacement.
