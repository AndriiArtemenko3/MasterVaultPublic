--- Internal note (JB) --- 2025-06-05 10:15 PT
#LS60111, 2025-02-06, LS-CHR-001-BLK. squeak = recline tension bolt underneath the seat pan backing off, common after a few months of daily use, needs a quarter turn tightening with a hex key not a full retorque. armrest cracking at 4 months is early tho, sending LS-ACC-009 pads free (29.00), well under self-approve.

--- Message 2 (agent, public reply) --- 2025-06-05 11:02 PT
Sorry to hear about both of these, Alma, and sorry in advance for the slightly technical fix on the squeak. Underneath the seat, right where the recline mechanism meets the base, there's a tension bolt that backs off a little over months of regular use. A quarter turn clockwise with a 5mm hex key should quiet it right down, no need to overtighten past that. On the armrests, four months is earlier than we'd expect for cracking at the edges, so I'm sending a replacement set of the Rowan armrest pads (LS-ACC-009) at no charge. They'll ship from the warehouse this week.

Jonah
Larkstead Support
