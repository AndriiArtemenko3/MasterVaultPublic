--- Message 1 (customer) --- 2025-12-15 19:52 PT
Lamp's been flickering the last two nights once it dims down for sleep mode. I messed around with the app settings and turning off the sleep-mode dimming schedule entirely seems to stop it, brightness stays fixed instead of stepping down, but at least no more flicker. Is that actually fixing anything or am I just getting lucky.

--- Internal note (CM) --- 2025-12-15 20:10 PT
order confirmed, #LS31710, placed 2025-11-20, 1x LS-LMP-001. app update history shows firmware v1.2 auto-installed 2025-12-12. first customer to report the disable-dim workaround. logging it for the team.

--- Message 2 (agent, public reply) --- 2025-12-15 20:24 PT
Hi Kit. Your order #LS31710 shows firmware v1.2, auto-installed on 2025-12-12, matching other flicker reports we've had recently.

You're not getting lucky. Disabling the sleep-mode dimming schedule in the app is a real workaround, it stops the lamp from stepping down to the low brightness where the flicker happens. The tradeoff is your brightness stays fixed instead of easing down at night.

I've logged this for our team to pass along to other affected customers while we work with the firmware vendor on a permanent fix.

--- Message 3 (customer) --- 2025-12-15 20:31 PT
ok good to know, ill leave it off for now then. thanks for the quick reply
