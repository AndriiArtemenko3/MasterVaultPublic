## Why we keep a parts shelf

Q: Why sell individual replacement parts instead of just replacing the whole desk or chair?
A: The catalog stays small on purpose: one desk platform, one chair, and a parts shelf deep enough that most repairs don't need a full replacement at all. If a single piece wears out or fails, you can usually order just that piece instead of the entire item, which is deliberate rather than something we back into because we're a small company.

Q: Do I need to know the exact part number to order the right thing?
A: It helps, but it's not required. Tell us what broke and what it's attached to, the desk, the chair, or the lamp, and support can confirm the right part before you buy anything.

## What's on the shelf

Q: What replacement parts are available right now?
A: Six cover most repairs. The Birch motor control box (LS-ACC-010) is 89.00. Birch leveling feet, sold as a set (LS-ACC-011), are 15.00. The Rowan caster set, five soft wheels (LS-ACC-008), is 24.00, and the Rowan armrest pad pair (LS-ACC-009) is 29.00. The Heron desk-clamp kit (LS-ACC-013) is 16.00, and the Vireo power adapter (LS-ACC-012) is 22.00.

Q: Are any of these sold outside the Larkstead storefront?
A: No. They're built to our exact frame and hardware dimensions, so they're direct-only.

## Before you order

Q: My part failed pretty fast. Should I just buy the replacement anyway?
A: Contact support first with your #LS order number so we can check whether it's a fit problem, a one-off defect, or normal wear over time. Ordering it yourself is always an option, but a quick check first can save you the cost if it's something we should be handling instead.

Q: How fast do these ship once ordered?
A: Same as any regular order, usually within two business days from the Portland warehouse. None of these six parts need special freight handling, so there's no separate wait for a replacement versus a first-time order.
