## Picking up your order

Q: How do I know my order is ready for pickup?
A: You'll get an email titled "Ready for pickup" with your #LS order number in the subject line. Small items like mats and cable kits are usually ready the same afternoon if you ordered before noon, while desks, chairs, and bundles take an extra day so the warehouse team can stage the cartons properly.

Q: What are your pickup hours?
A: Monday through Friday, 10am to 6pm. We added Saturday hours this spring, 11am to 4pm, for folks who can't get away during the week.

Q: Where exactly do I go?
A: The Larkstead showroom in NW Portland. Head to the pickup counter at the back of the floor, past the chair display wall. There's metered street parking out front, and the lot next door opens up after 5pm on weekdays.

Q: What do I need to bring?
A: A photo ID that matches the name on the order. If someone else is picking up for you, reply to the ready email with their name before they arrive. Otherwise the counter staff can't release it, no exceptions.

## Holds and cancellations

Q: How long will you hold my order?
A: 7 days from the date of the ready email. Weekends count too.

Q: What happens after 7 days?
A: We cancel the pickup, refund the order in full to your original payment method, and put the stock back on the shelf for the next customer, which means if you're on the fence about a color or a size you should decide before day 7 rather than after, since we can't promise the same item will still be sitting there once it's gone back into general inventory.

Q: Can I ask for more time?
A: Reach out before day 7 and we can usually stretch it a few extra days for small items. Desks and bundles are trickier. The backroom is small, and one held desk carton takes up space three other orders could use.

Q: Can I switch a pickup order to shipping instead?
A: Yes. Email support with your #LS number before day 7 and we'll rebook it for delivery. Standard shipping rates and zone charges apply once it converts.
