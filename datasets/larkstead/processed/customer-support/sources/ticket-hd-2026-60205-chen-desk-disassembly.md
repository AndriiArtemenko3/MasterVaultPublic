--- Message 2 (agent, public reply) --- 2026-03-05 13:05 PT
Hi Ivy, good news, there's no torque wrench needed here at all. One quick note, I couldn't find #LS20214 on our end, but your account shows order #LS60214 from 2025-09-10 with the 72 inch frame, so I'm working from that one.

The frame bolts just get snugged down by hand with the Allen key from the box, tight enough that nothing wiggles, no foot-pounds or newton-meters to hit. To take it apart, reverse our assembly guide from 2024: loosen the crossbar-to-leg bolts a little on each side first, left, right, left, right, so the frame comes apart evenly instead of racking, then remove them once it's loose everywhere. Relocation guide's attached too, covering the motor cable and control box before the frame bolts. Given the 72 inch frame's weight, budget a second person for the lift.
Celeste
Larkstead Support
