## Free shipping

Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: Yes. Orders of 75.00 or more ship free. That threshold took effect on 2024-03-01, so this page reflects the current cutoff, not whatever you might have seen mentioned somewhere older.

Q: Does free shipping cover everything in my cart?
A: It covers our light and medium items only, things like the Wren laptop stand, desk mats, and cable accessories, but it does not apply to heavy items such as our desks and chairs, which always bill the zone shipping rate no matter how large the rest of your order is, so if your cart mixes a chair with a few small accessories don't be surprised to see the chair's shipping line stay on the invoice even while the accessories ship free.

## Shipping zones and rates

Q: How much does shipping cost if my order doesn't qualify for free shipping?
A: It depends on your zone and the weight class of what you're ordering. Folks in Oregon, Washington, Idaho, and Nevada are in Zone US-1: 5.95 for light items, 12.95 for medium.

Q: What about heavy items like a desk or a chair?
A: Heavy items always bill a flat zone rate, free shipping or not. Zone US-1 is 49.00, US-2 is 69.00, US-3 is 89.00, and US-4 is 109.00.

Q: I'm in Canada. Do these rates apply to me?
A: No, Canada ships on its own rate table. Ask support for a quote before you check out if you want the number ahead of time.

## Delivery time

Q: How long will it take to get here?
A: Standard transit is 3-5 business days from the warehouse once your order ships. Desks and bundles sometimes take an extra day to leave the dock because of carton size, but the transit clock itself doesn't change once it starts.

Q: Can I track my package?
A: Yep, you'll get a tracking link by email the same day your order ships.

Q: What if my package is late?
A: Reach out to support with your order number and we'll look into it with the carrier.
