Hi Ivy, that sinking overnight is the gas lift cylinder losing pressure on
its own, and you're right that it's not something you're doing, it just
happens as a cylinder ages or if one shipped with a slow seal issue. This is
covered under our 1 year standard warranty, effective 2024-01-15, and your
chair from 2024-04-11 is well inside that window. I'm sending a replacement
cylinder along with a prepaid return label so you can send the old one back
to us once the new one's installed, no charge either way. The swap only
takes a few minutes and I'll include a short instruction sheet in the box.
