--- Message 2 (agent, public reply) --- 2025-08-14 17:10 PT
Hi Ruben, I understand why the price gap stings on a student budget, and I looked into this before answering rather than giving you a flat no. Our match policy, in force since 2025-05-19, covers identical-spec list prices from US-based sellers within 14 days of purchase, which your order falls inside. The ErgoNest desk isn't identical spec though: a laminate composite top instead of birch veneer, a lighter duty-cycle lift motor, and no replacement-parts catalog at all, so a broken part means buying a whole new desk rather than a 15.00 leveling foot set or an 89.00 motor control box from us. Those differences are why I can't apply the match here.

Priya
Larkstead Support
