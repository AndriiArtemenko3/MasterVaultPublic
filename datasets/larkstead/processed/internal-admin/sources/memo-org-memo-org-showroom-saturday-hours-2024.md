## Content

To: all staff
From: Mara Voss
Date: 2024-04-08
Subject: Saturday showroom hours starting April 20

Showroom adds Saturdays starting April 20. 10am to 4pm, same as the weekday hours we already run.

We keep getting pickup requests for the weekend, and NW Portland foot traffic's been picking up now that the weather's turned, so this is a six-week trial before anyone decides whether it becomes permanent. Six Saturdays, then we look at the numbers.

Rotation for the trial: Priya takes April 20 and May 4. Jonah takes April 27 and May 11. I've got May 18 and May 25. Swap between yourselves if something comes up, just tell Ana so payroll matches.

Same walkthrough as a weekday open. Doors unlock at 9:45. Alarm code stays the same. No new stock goes on the floor for this, just the regular hours extended by a day.

Hank, the loading dock stays closed Saturdays. This is showroom only, no receiving.

Ana: add the two Saturday shifts to the payroll calendar this week.

Sofia, if this holds past the trial I want a line in the next newsletter. Not yet.

Who wants Saturdays on a permanent basis, once the six weeks are up?

M
