## Content

ENGAGEMENT LETTER

This engagement letter ("Engagement Letter") is made 2024-06-03 (the "Effective Date") between Larkstead Goods Company, LLC ("Client") and Colefield & Tran LLP ("Firm", VEN-10), and restates the scope of the Firm's retainer representation of Client, ongoing since the relationship began 2022-06-10.

1. Definitions
1.1 "Retainer Scope" means the categories of legal work described in Section 2, billed first against the monthly retainer described in Section 3.
1.2 "MSA Template" means the business-to-business master services agreement template the Firm is drafting for Client's use with prospective business accounts, deliverable under Section 2.1.
1.3 "Standard Hourly Rates" means the rates set in Section 3.2, applicable to work outside the Retainer Scope and to hours beyond the monthly retainer.

2. Scope of engagement
2.1 The Firm will draft the MSA Template, covering standard terms for seat pricing, discount tiers, and payment terms, with delivery targeted before 2024-07-01.
2.2 The Firm will advise Client on employment matters as they arise, including hiring documentation, contractor classification, and termination procedure.
2.3 The Firm will review vendor contracts and advise on general corporate matters at Client's request.
2.4 Litigation is outside the Retainer Scope. Representing Client in a lawsuit or arbitration requires a separate engagement letter.

3. Fees
3.1 Client pays a monthly retainer of 1,500.00, covering up to 5 hours of Firm time within the Retainer Scope each calendar month, whether or not Client uses all 5 hours.
3.2 Hours beyond the monthly retainer, and any matter outside the Retainer Scope, bill at Standard Hourly Rates of 310.00 per hour for partner time and 185.00 per hour for associate time.
3.3 The Firm invoices monthly in arrears on Net-30 terms, itemizing hours worked by matter.
3.4 Retainer hours unused in a given month do not carry forward to the next month.

4. Confidentiality
4.1 Each party shall keep the other's non-public business information confidential and use it only to perform this Engagement Letter, a duty that survives termination.

5. Term and termination
5.1 This Engagement Letter runs month to month from the Effective Date. Either party may terminate it on 30 days' written notice, without cause.
5.2 Termination does not affect fees already earned under Section 3 or work in progress on the MSA Template if termination falls before delivery, which the Firm will complete and bill on its usual terms unless Client instructs otherwise in writing.

Larkstead Goods Company, LLC          Colefield & Tran LLP
