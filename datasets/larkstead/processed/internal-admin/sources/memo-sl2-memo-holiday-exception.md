# Sl2 Memo Holiday Exception

## Summary

To: Priya Raman, support team From: Mara Voss Date: 2025-10-27 Subject: Holiday returns window, 30 to 45 days Return window goes to 45 days. Effective for anything delivered on or after 2025-11-03.

## Content

To: Priya Raman, support team
From: Mara Voss
Date: 2025-10-27
Subject: Holiday returns window, 30 to 45 days

Return window goes to 45 days. Effective for anything delivered on or
after 2025-11-03.

Holiday shopping starts early and gifts sit under a tree for weeks.
Thirty days from delivery cuts most gift returns off before anyone even
opens the box. That's bad timing on our part, not the customer's.

This is a holiday exception. Not a permanent policy change, not yet. We
watch it through the season and decide in January whether 45 days
sticks.

Restocking fee does not move. Still 10% on opened, non-defective
returns. No changes there.

Nothing else in the returns policy moves either. Refund timing stays 5
business days from warehouse receipt. Defective items still ship back
free. Only the window number changes.

Priya: update the site FAQ and both Helprise macros, RET-01 and RET-02,
before 2025-11-03. Every one of those needs to say 45 days by then. A
customer who reads 30 on our page and hears something different from an
agent will not read that as a nice surprise.

Loop me in if the exception creates a backlog. I would rather hear about
it in November than in January.

What does 15 extra days cost us in January?

M
