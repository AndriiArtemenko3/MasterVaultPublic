## Content

Compliance note: Vireo power adapter electrical safety certification
Date: 2024-09-24
Author: Ana Petrova
Status: informational
Next review: 2025-09-24

Position
Cascade Compliance Testing issued the electrical safety certification for the Vireo smart desk lamp's power adapter on 2024-09-11, ahead of the 2024-10-15 launch of LS-LMP-001. The adapter itself is supplied by Meridian Components (VEN-07) out of Taiwan, under the standing electronic parts contract, Net-45. Original certified sample, three production units, all passed on first submission, no resubmission needed. If a future adapter revision changes the transformer, the output rating, or the connector, then a fresh certification report is required from Cascade before that revision ships, per the terms Meridian agreed to when the contract started 2024-02-01.

Obligations
| obligation | detail | where handled |
|---|---|---|
| electrical safety cert | independent lab report, adapter model shared by LS-LMP-001 and the LS-ACC-012 replacement unit | Cascade Compliance Testing, report on file |
| certificate retention | original report plus raw test data kept 7 years from issue date | Ana Petrova, compliance archive, shared drive |
| revision trigger | any transformer, output rating, or connector change from Meridian Components requires a new cert before shipment | Ana Petrova and Dmitri Okafor, vendor coordination |

Open items
- LS-ACC-012, the replacement adapter, launched 2025-02-10 using the same certified spec as the original LS-LMP-001 adapter, so no new cert was triggered. Confirmed against the Cascade report on file. Owner AP, closing note only, nothing pending.
- Meridian's inspection cadence at their Taiwan facility isn't spelled out in the current contract language. If the contract comes up for renewal, then push to get an inspection clause added rather than rely on the cert alone. Owner AP, check at next contract review, no date set yet.
