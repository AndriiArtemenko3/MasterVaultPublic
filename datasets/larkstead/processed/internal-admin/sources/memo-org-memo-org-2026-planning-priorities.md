# Memo Org 2026 Planning Priorities

## Summary

To: all staff From: Mara Voss Date: 2026-01-08 Subject: 2026 priorities Three things for this year. In order.

## Content

To: all staff
From: Mara Voss
Date: 2026-01-08
Subject: 2026 priorities

Three things for this year. In order.

One: grow B2B without growing headcount past what we can support well. Tom and Yuki carry the pipeline, and I'm not adding a third seat to sales until one of them is stretched past their current load, not before.

Two: keep the catalog small. One desk platform, one chair, a parts shelf deep enough people repair instead of replace, that's the whole pitch, and I'm not diluting it with a second chair or a cheaper desk tier just because a prospect asks for one.

Three: hold response times steady as ticket volume climbs. Priya's team added Celeste in March 2025, and I don't want that gain eaten by a bigger queue this year. If wait times start slipping, tell me before the customer does.

Juniper Cowork and Beacon Legal are both live right now, 55 seats and 18 seats between them, and if both close this quarter that's 73 new seats to install on top of whatever's already scheduled.

Do we have the install and warehouse capacity to handle both rollouts back to back, or does one of them need to stagger into Q2?

M
