That number is the reason I'm writing you instead of approving it myself. 1,318.80 sits at or above the 1,000.00 CEO threshold in the expense policy effective 2025-07-01, and it's outside my own approval band of 500.00 to 999.99. It needs your signature, not mine.

If you sign off, the combined discount on the bundle line is the 15% volume tier, 6,594.00, plus the 3% exception, 1,318.80, which is 7,912.80 off in total. The bundle line then nets to 36,047.20.

Tom is waiting on this before he can get proposal v2 in front of Sam Ortiz. He's under some time pressure: Cobalt is holding an ErgoNest counter-offer at 37,920.00.

Ana
