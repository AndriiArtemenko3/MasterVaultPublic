## Content

SUPPLY AGREEMENT RENEWAL

This renewal ("Renewal") is made 2025-01-13 (the "Effective Date") between
Larkstead Goods Company, LLC ("Client") and GreenCrate Packaging ("Vendor",
VEN-05), and renews the supply agreement between the parties dated
2022-08-01 (the "Original Agreement") covering cartons, molded pulp
inserts, and mailers. Client's file review for this Renewal was completed
by Ana Petrova (AP) on 2025-01-06, with the Vendor's updated certificate of
insurance received 2025-01-10 and the packet signed by Mara Voss the same
week.

1. Definitions
1.1 "Renewal Term" means the period from the Effective Date through
    2027-01-12.
1.2 "Recycled-Content Minimum" means the floor share of post-consumer
    recycled fiber content Vendor must maintain across every carton and
    mailer SKU supplied under this Renewal.
1.3 "Redesign [[support]]" means the engineering, sampling, and drop-test
    coordination Vendor provides in connection with Client's anticipated
    corner redesign of the LS-DSK-001 desk carton line.
1.4 "Standard Carton" means the current flat-corner desk carton design in
    production as of the Effective Date, prior to any Redesign Support
    deliverable reaching production.

2. Recycled content
2.1 Vendor shall maintain a Recycled-Content Minimum of no less than 80%
    post-consumer recycled fiber content across every carton and mailer
    SKU supplied under this Renewal, effective from the Effective Date.
2.2 Vendor shall provide a mill certificate confirming the Recycled-Content
    Minimum with each new production lot on request.

3. Redesign support
3.1 Vendor shall provide Redesign Support at no additional charge for the
    duration of the Renewal Term, covering sample production, drop-test
    coordination, and print-run scheduling for the LS-DSK-001 corner
    redesign once Client's engineering team opens the project.
3.2 Redesign Support does not include tooling costs for a change outside
    the corner geometry; any such change is priced separately by written
    change order.

4. [[pricing]]
4.1 Pricing for the Standard Carton continues unchanged from the Original
    Agreement at 2,150.00 per thousand units, matching the per-unit rate
    invoiced on INV-GCP-2024-058.
4.2 Pulp insert sets continue unchanged at 1,200.00 per thousand units.
4.3 Once a redesigned desk carton produced under Section 3 enters
    production, pricing for that carton restates to 2,650.00 per thousand
    units, replacing Section 4.1 for that SKU only.
4.4 [[payment-terms]] remain Net-30 per the Original Agreement, unchanged by
    this Renewal.

5. Order of precedence
5.1 This Renewal controls over the Original Agreement where the two
    conflict. All other terms of the Original Agreement dated 2022-08-01
    stand.
