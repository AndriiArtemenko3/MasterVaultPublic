## Content

To: all staff
From: Ana Petrova
Date: 2025-02-10
Subject: Keycard changeover, Feb 17

Lovejoy's swapping the building's keycard system the week of Feb 17. New fobs replace the old badges, and the old ones stop working at 6pm on the 16th, no grace period, so grab your new fob from me before you leave that Friday. No exceptions.

If you're not in the office Friday, come find me Monday morning before the old badge deactivates on you mid-shift. I'll have a stack ready at the front desk.

Parking: spots 1 through 4 in the lot are assigned now instead of first-come. Mara gets 1, Dmitri 2, Hank 3 for the truck, and 4 rotates between whoever's driving in that week for warehouse runs. Everyone else is still street parking, same as before.

After-hours and weekends now require a badge-in at the side door, since the old buzzer system goes away with the changeover. No propped doors, and no letting yourselves out through the alarm sensor to avoid tripping it, that's how we got the false alarm call back in January.

Lost or dead fobs replace for 15.00 each, which I'll deduct from the next paycheck if the old one isn't turned in within a week. If your fob stops reading and it's not a dead battery, email me and I'll flag it to Lovejoy directly rather than you calling the property line yourself.

Rent notice for March goes out the 1st as usual. This changeover doesn't touch that.

Ana
