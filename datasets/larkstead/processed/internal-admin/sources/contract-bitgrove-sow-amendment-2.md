## Content

AMENDMENT NO. 2 TO STATEMENT OF WORK

This amendment ("Amendment") is made 2026-01-26 (the "Amendment Date") between Larkstead Goods Company, LLC ("Client") and Bitgrove Labs ("Vendor", VEN-03), and amends the statement of work under the master services agreement dated 2024-03-01 (the "Master Agreement"). Client's internal spend approval was recorded 2026-01-23 under the expense policy effective 2025-07-01, this spend exceeding the 1,000.00 CEO approval threshold under that policy.

1. Definitions
1.1 "Hotfix" means firmware release v1.3 for the Vireo smart desk lamp (LS-LMP-001), correcting the sleep-mode dimming defect on units built with Meridian PCB rev C.
1.2 "Rollback Image" means the signed firmware v1.1 image pinned to the OTA channel for Meridian PCB rev C serials, delivered by Vendor before the Amendment Date and already in production use.
1.3 "Monitoring Period" means the 90 consecutive days following the release date of the Hotfix.

2. Scope
2.1 Vendor delivers the Hotfix no later than 2026-02-17.
2.2 The Rollback Image is accepted by Client as delivered and requires no further fee under this Amendment.
2.3 Vendor provides defect monitoring and triage through the Monitoring Period, including review of any recurring sleep-dim symptoms on Meridian PCB rev C serials.
2.4 Vendor confirms the Hotfix removes the need for the Rollback Image pin once rolled out, and coordinates the pin removal with Client's operations team.

3. Fee and invoicing
3.1 Client pays Vendor a fixed fee of 4,800.00 for the work described in Section 2.
3.2 Vendor invoices the fee as INV-BGL-2026-006, payable on Net-30 terms per the Master Agreement's [[payment-terms]] for Vendor.
3.3 No additional fee applies for the Rollback Image already delivered under Section 2.2.

4. Order of precedence
4.1 This Amendment controls over the Master Agreement where the two conflict. All other terms of the Master Agreement remain in force.
