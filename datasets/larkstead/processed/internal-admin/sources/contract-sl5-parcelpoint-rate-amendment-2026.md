## Content

AMENDMENT NO. 2 TO MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This amendment ("Amendment") is made 2026-03-27 (the "Signing Date") between Larkstead Goods Company, LLC ("Client") and ParcelPoint Fulfillment ("Vendor", VEN-04), and resolves a billing dispute arising under Amendment No. 1 (2025-07-28) to the master services agreement dated 2023-11-01 (together, the "Master Agreement").

1. Definitions

1.1 "Disputed Charges" means the dimensional weight adjustment lines billed on INV-PPF-2026-002 (1320.00) and INV-PPF-2026-003 (1072.50), totaling 2392.50, both calculated using a dimensional divisor of 139 on heavy weight-class cartons.

1.2 "Restated Exhibit B" means the weight-billing exhibit as clarified by this Amendment, replacing Exhibit B of Amendment No. 1 in full.

2. Restated Exhibit B

2.1 Heavy weight-class cartons bill at actual scale weight. No dimensional-weight calculation applies to a heavy carton under any circumstance.

2.2 Dimensional weight, calculated with a divisor of 166, applies to light and medium parcels only.

2.3 Billing a heavy carton with any divisor other than actual scale weight, including but not limited to the divisor-139 method used on the Disputed Charges, is barred under this Master Agreement.

2.4 This Restated Exhibit B applies retroactively to 2026-01-12, the date Vendor assumed full outbound fulfillment under Amendment No. 1.

3. Resolution of Disputed Charges

3.1 Vendor waives the 1320.00 open balance remaining on INV-PPF-2026-002.

3.2 Vendor credits 1072.50 against INV-PPF-2026-003, reducing its net balance to 15809.95.

3.3 INV-PPF-2026-003's due date is unchanged at 2026-04-03.

3.4 No further dimensional weight adjustment will appear on any heavy-carton line on any future invoice.

4. Effective date

4.1 This Amendment is effective 2026-04-01, notwithstanding the retroactive scope of Section 2.4.

5. Order of precedence

5.1 This Amendment controls over Amendment No. 1 and the Master Agreement where any of the three conflict. All other terms of the Master Agreement and Amendment No. 1 stand.
