## Content

Policy: Employee product purchase
Effective: 2024-10-15
Supersedes: none
Approved by: Mara Voss
Prepared by: Ana Petrova
Applies to: all employees, full and part time; excludes contract staff, since Ray Lindqvist and any other contractor is not a Larkstead employee under this policy

1. Discount
Employees receive 30% off the current list price on any Larkstead SKU, requested by emailing Ana Petrova with the SKU and quantity rather than ordering through the public storefront, since the public checkout has no employee code field. Ana keys the order into Shopstack at the discounted price and confirms the total before charging the card on file. For any single order over 300.00, Ana can split the charge across up to three pay periods by payroll deduction if the employee asks.

2. Large-item cap
Two large items per rolling 12-month period. A large item means any Birch standing desk, any Rowan task chair, or any bundle in the LS-BDL series. The cap resets on a rolling basis from each purchase date, not on the calendar year. Accessories, the Wren stand, the Heron arms, and the Vireo lamp are not large items and do not count against the cap, though the same 30% discount and the terms below still apply to them.

3. No resale
Purchases under this policy are for the employee's personal use or their immediate household. Items bought at the employee rate must not be resold, listed for sale anywhere, or transferred to a friend, family member, or business contact for money. Ana may ask where a large item ended up if she sees the same employee buying the same SKU twice inside a short window. A pattern that looks like resale ends the benefit.

4. No stacking
The discount does not combine with any other discount, promotional code, or price adjustment offered to the public. One rate, one order, full stop.

5. End of employment
A purchase already placed and paid for stands as is. The benefit itself ends on the employee's last day, and any order placed after that date goes through at the regular public price.
