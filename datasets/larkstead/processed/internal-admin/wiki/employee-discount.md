## Definition
**Operating:** Employees receive a discount of 30% off the current list price on any Larkstead SKU. This employee discount is processed in the internal Shopstack ordering system, where the discounted price is keyed in by authorized personnel, such as Ana.

## Main Insights
- Employees can benefit from a 30% discount on any product listed under a Larkstead SKU.
- The discount is applied directly through the Shopstack system, ensuring a seamless purchasing experience for employees.
- Ana is responsible for entering the discount in the system, highlighting the internal management of the discount process.

## Why It Compounds
The employee discount program not only serves as an incentive for employees, enhancing their overall job satisfaction and loyalty, but it can also improve workplace morale. When employees feel valued through savings on products, they are likely to perform better and stay longer with the company, ultimately benefiting overall productivity and reducing turnover costs.
