## Definition
**Operating:** Requesting time off refers to the formal process employees follow to inform their managers of their intention to take leave from work. Proper notice is crucial for effective workforce planning and operational continuity.

## Main Insights
- For time off of 1 or 2 days, employees should email their manager at least 3 business days in advance.
- For time off of 3 or more consecutive days, employees are required to provide at least 10 business days notice.
- Adequate notice allows teams to adjust schedules and maintain productivity during an employee's absence.

## Why It Compounds
Requesting time off is essential not only for individual work-life balance but also for the overall health and efficiency of the organization. When employees follow the established protocols for requesting leave, it helps managers plan accordingly, ensuring that workloads are manageable and that other team members are not overwhelmed. This practice fosters a positive work environment where both employee needs and organizational goals are respected.
