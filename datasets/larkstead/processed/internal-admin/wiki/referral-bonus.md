## Definition
**Operating:** Larkstead currently has a Referral Bonus program where current employees are rewarded for referring successful job candidates. When a referred candidate is hired, the referring employee receives a bonus of $500. 

## Main Insights
- The bonus is only paid if both the new hire and the referring employee are still employed with Larkstead on the 90th day after the hiring date.  
- Payment of the referral bonus occurs during the payroll run that follows this 90-day period.  
- If either party (the new hire or the referring employee) leaves the company before the 90-day mark, the bonus is forfeited.  

## Why It Compounds
The Referral Bonus program not only incentivizes employees to leverage their networks in finding suitable candidates but also fosters a sense of ownership and commitment among staff to help cultivate a strong and cohesive workforce. This alignment of interests encourages a culture of collaboration, leading to potentially higher retention rates and improved overall employee morale.
