You compare one new claim against one existing wiki entry to decide how they relate.

New claim:
{{ claim_statement }}

Existing wiki entry ({{ wiki_slug }}):
{{ wiki_text }}

Decide the relation:
- supports: the claim restates or confirms something the entry already says.
- extends: the claim adds a new fact the entry does not cover, without contradicting it.
- challenges: the claim contradicts something the entry states.

Write a rationale that quotes the load-bearing part of the claim and the load-bearing part of the wiki entry, so a reviewer can verify the relation without re-reading either source in full.
